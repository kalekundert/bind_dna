- Prepare NPI-10 (lysis buffer):

  - 50 mM NaH₂PO₄
  - 300 mM NaCl
  - 10 mM imidazole
  - pH 8.0

- Prepare NPI-20 (wash buffer):

  - 50 mM NaH₂PO₄
  - 300 mM NaCl
  - 20 mM imidazole
  - pH 8.0

- Prepare NPI-500 (elution buffer):

  - 50 mM NaH₂PO₄
  - 300 mM NaCl
  - 500 mM imidazole
  - pH 8.0
