$ stepwise detect_rnase_h

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. Prepare 5 µM o210-o212:

    - 19 µL nuclease-free water
    -  1 µL 100 µM oligo

 3. Setup two blank wells (one will become a FAM
    positive control):

    - 10.5 µL nuclease-free water

 4. Setup control reactions:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume      3.3x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water         10.00 µL  33.00 µL
    o210,o211,o212        5 µM   0.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                                10.50 µL  10.00 µL/rxn

 5. Setup an RNase H assay for PURExpress:

    Reagent                     Stock    Volume      3.3x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                 2.80 µL   9.24 µL
    A                                   4.00 µL  13.20 µL
    B                                   3.00 µL   9.90 µL
    RNase inhibitor (murine)  40 U/µL   0.20 µL   0.66 µL
    o210,o211,o212               5 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                       10.50 µL  10.00 µL/rxn

 6. Setup an RNase H assay for PUREfrex:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume      3.3x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          3.50 µL  11.55 µL
    Solution I                   5.00 µL  16.50 µL
    Solution II                  0.50 µL   1.65 µL
    Solution III                 1.00 µL   3.30 µL
    o210,o211,o212        5 µM   0.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                                10.50 µL  10.00 µL/rxn

 7. Incubate at 37°C for 30 min.

 8. Prepare 5x DNAzyme buffer:

    Reagent        Stock     Volume
    ───────────────────────────────
    water                 259.00 µL
    Tris pH=8.5  1000 mM  100.00 µL
    NaCl         5000 mM   40.00 µL
    MgCl₂        2000 mM    1.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────
                          400.00 µL

 9. Setup the following reactions in a black, opaque-
    bottomed 96-well plate:

    Reagent               Stock     Volume       9.9x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water           69.40 µL  687.06 µL
    DNAzyme buffer           5x   20.00 µL  198.00 µL
    o213                 100 µM    0.10 µL    0.99 µL
    samples                       10.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 100.00 µL   89.50 µL/rxn

10. Prepare a FAM control:

    10 µM o42:
    - 9 µL nuclease-free water
    - 1 µL 100 µM o42

    FAM control:
    - 68.50 µL nuclease-free water
    - 20.00 µL 5x DNAzyme buffer
    -  1.00 µL 10 µM o42
    - 10.50 µL blank

11. Measure flourescence (ex: 450, em: 521) in a
    plate reader for 30 min at 30°C without shaking.
