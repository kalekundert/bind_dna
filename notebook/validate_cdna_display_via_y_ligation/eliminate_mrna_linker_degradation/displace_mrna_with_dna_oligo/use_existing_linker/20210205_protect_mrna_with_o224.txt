$ stepwise protect_mrna_with_o224

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    mRNA (f85):            +  −  −  −  −  −  −  −   −    −    −
    poly-A linker (o129):  −  +  −  −  −  −  −  −   −    −    −
    mRNA + linker (f89):   −  −  −  +  +  +  +  +   +    +    +
    dna/dna oligo (o224):  0  0  1  0  0  1  1  10  10  100  100
    RNase H:               −  −  −  −  +  −  +  −   +    −    +

    dna/dna oligo values are molar ratios relative to mRNA+linker

 3. Setup 1 annealing reaction [1]:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          6.90 µL
    PBS                    10x   1.20 µL
    f85                   5 µM   3.00 µL
    o129                 10 µM   0.90 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                12.00 µL

 4. Perform the annealing reaction:

    - Incubate at 95°C for 2 min.
    - Cool at room temperature.

 5. Setup 1 ligation reaction:

    Reagent                 Stock     Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water             82.50 µL
    T4 DNA ligase buffer      10x   12.00 µL
    BSA                      0.1%   12.00 µL
    T4 RNA ligase         40 U/µL    1.50 µL
    annealed mRNA/linker  1.25 µM   12.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────
                                   120.00 µL

 6. Incubate the ligation reaction as follows:

    - 25°C for 10 min.
    - 65°C for 10 min.

 7. Remove unligated linker by ultrafiltration:

    - Bring reaction to 500 µL with 8M urea.
    - Load onto a 100 kDa MWCO spin-filter [2].
    - Spin 14000g, 15 min.
    - Wash with 500 µL 8M urea.
    - Wash with 500 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Wash with water again.
    - Wash with water again [3].
    - Invert the filter into a clean tube and spin 
      1000g, 2 min to collect ligated product in a 
      volume of ≈15 µL.

 8. Label the product: f89

 9. Perform a serial dilution [4]:

    - Put 10.00 μL 100 µM o224 in a tube.

    - Put 9.00 μL water in 3 adjacent tubes.

    - Transfer 1.00 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 3 10-fold dilutions.

10. Setup 4 annealing reactions:

    Reagent    Stock   Volume     4.4x
    ──────────────────────────────────
    PBS          10x  0.30 µL  1.32 µL
    f89         1 µM  1.35 µL  5.94 µL
    o224     0-100µM  1.35 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────
                      3.00 µL  1.65 µL/rxn

11. Incubate at 95°C for 2 min, then let cool at room
    temperature.

12. Setup 4 +RNase H reactions [5] [6]:

    Reagent                  Stock   Volume      4.4x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water             4.28 µL  18.83 µL
    RNase H buffer             10x  0.60 µL   2.64 µL
    f89 + o224                1 µM  1.00 µL
    RNase H              5000 U/mL  0.12 µL   0.53 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    6.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

13. Setup 4 −RNase H reactions:

    Reagent              Stock   Volume      4.4x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water         4.40 µL  19.36 µL
    RNase H buffer         10x  0.60 µL   2.64 µL
    f89 + o224            1 µM  1.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                6.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

14. Incubate at 37°C for 20 min [7].

15. Prepare 11 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent            Stock    Volume     16.5x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
    sample buffer         2x   5.00 µL  82.50 µL
    sample         200 ng/µL   5.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
                              10.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

16. Run a gel:

    - Gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    - Buffer: 1x TBE
    - Load 10 µL of each sample.
    - Run at 180V for 40 min.

17. Stain gel with GelGreen:

    - Submerge gel in ≈50 mL 3x GelGreen [8].
    - Shake gently for 30 min.

18. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40

Notes:
[1] Using 0.6x linker reduces the amount of unligated 
    linker, see expt #1.

[2] Amicon UFC510024

[3] Final urea concentration: ~200 µM

[4] The final concentrations will be:
    1  1.000000e+02 µM
    2  1.000000e+01 µM
    3  1.000000e+00 µM
    4  0.000000e+00 µM

[5] mRNA volume and concentration derived from expt
    #18.  Expts #61,62,65 have a different ratio, but
    I'm not sure why.

[6] 100 U/mL RNase H final; need at least 8 U/mL to
    digest all template, see expt #77.

[7] https://tinyurl.com/y2deaw9k

[8] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution 
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye 
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

    Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
