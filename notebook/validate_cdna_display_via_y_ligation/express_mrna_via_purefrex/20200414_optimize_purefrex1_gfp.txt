$ sw rnasezap
$ sw serial_dilution 2.6 '10000 nM' to 100 7 -0 -m f15 -d 'nuclease-free water'
$ sw ivtt f15 -p purefrex1 -v 2.5 -n 8 -c 2000 -C 10000
$ sw gels/gel bolt/ivtt 8

1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
   (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

2. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

   - Put 4.85 μL 10000 nM f15 in a tube.
   - Put 2.60 μL nuclease-free water in 7 adjacent
     tubes.
   - Transfer 2.25 μL between each tube *except the
     last* to make 7 2.2-fold dilutions.

3. Setup 8 PUREfrex 1.0 (GeneFrontier PF001)
   reactions [2]:

   Reagent          Stock   Volume      8.8x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
   water                   0.50 µL   4.40 µL
   solution I              1.25 µL  11.00 µL
   solution II             0.12 µL   1.10 µL
   solution III            0.12 µL   1.10 µL
   f15           10000 nM  0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────
                           2.50 µL   2.00 µL/rxn

   - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
     on ice.
   - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
   - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

4. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

5. Prepare 8 samples for SDS PAGE:

   Reagent                                    Stock    Volume       12x
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                              4.00 µL  48.00 µL
   Bolt LDS loading buffer (crystal violet)      4x   2.50 µL  30.00 µL
   Bolt reducing agent                          10x   1.00 µL  12.00 µL
   sample                                    160 nM   2.50 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                     10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

   - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

6. Run a gel:

   gel: 4−12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
   buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
   ladder: 5 µL SeeBlue Plus2 (Invitrogen LC5925)
   samples: 10 µL
   run: 165V for 42 min

7. Image with a laser scanner:

   lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
   filters:  518BP22  710BP40
