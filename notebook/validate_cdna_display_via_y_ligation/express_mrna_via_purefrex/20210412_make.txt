$ sw make f15

1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch
   (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

2. Setup 1 in vitro transcription reaction [1]:

   Reagent                  Stock    Volume
   ────────────────────────────────────────
   nuclease-free water              3.00 µL
   reaction buffer            10x   2.00 µL
   rNTP mix                100 mM   8.00 µL
   HiScribe T7                10x   2.00 µL
   f14                  200 ng/µL   5.00 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────
                                   20.00 µL

   - Mix reagents in the order given.
   - Ok to handle at room temperature.

3. Incubate at 37°C for 2 hours [2].

4. Remove unincorporated ribonucleotides using Zymo
   RNA Clean & Concentrator 25 spin columns.

5. Label the product: f15

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/y4a2j8w5

[2] Use a thermocycler to prevent evaporation.
