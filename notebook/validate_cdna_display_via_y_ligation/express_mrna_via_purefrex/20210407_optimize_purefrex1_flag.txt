 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    DNA template (f110) [nM]:   2   0     0    0    0   0   0   0
    mRNA template (f111) [nM]:  0  2000  796  317  126  50  20  0

 3. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 4.32 μL 10000 nM f111 in a tube.
    - Put 2.60 μL nuclease-free water in 6 adjacent
      tubes.
    - Transfer 1.72 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 6 2.5-fold dilutions.

 4. Dilute f110 to 10 nM so that it will have a final
    concentration of 2 nM in the following reaction.

 5. Setup 8 PUREfrex 1.0 (GeneFrontier PF001)
    reactions [2]:

    Reagent                       Stock   Volume      8.8x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                0.32 µL   2.86 µL
    solution I                           1.25 µL  11.00 µL
    solution II                          0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    solution III                         0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine     40 U/µL  0.05 µL   0.44 µL
    FluoroTect GreenLys                  0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    f111                       10000 nM  0.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         2.50 µL   2.00 µL/rxn

    - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
      on ice.
    - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
    - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

 6. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

 7. Add 0.5 µL 1 mg/mL RNase A to each reaction.

 8. Incubate at 37°C for 15 min.

 9. Prepare 8 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                  Stock    Volume       12x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                            5.50 µL  66.00 µL
    Bolt LDS sample buffer      4x   2.50 µL  30.00 µL
    Bolt reducing agent        10x   1.00 µL  12.00 µL
    sample                  160 nM   1.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

10. Run a gel:

    gel: 4−12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    ladder: 5 µL NativeMark unstained protein
      standard
    samples: 10 µL
    run: 165V for 42 min

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40
