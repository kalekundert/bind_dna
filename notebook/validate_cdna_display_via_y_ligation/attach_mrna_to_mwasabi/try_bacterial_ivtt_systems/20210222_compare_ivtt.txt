$ stepwise compare_ivtt

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    PURExpress:                    −  +  −  −  −
    PUREfrex:                      −  −  +  −  −
    NEBExpress:                    −  −  −  +  −
    Promega S30 extract (linear):  −  −  −  −  +
    mRNA + linker (f89):           +  +  +  +  +

 3. Setup 1 PURExpress (NEB E6800) reaction [1]:

    Reagent                    Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
    water                              1.20 µL
    solution A                         4.00 µL
    solution B                         3.00 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine  40 U/µL   0.20 µL
    f89                      1000 nM   1.60 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
                                      10.00 µL

    - Thaw all components on ice.
    - Mix A well; certain components may precipitate
      during storage.
    - Mix B gently; do not vortex.
    - Add A before B.

 4. Setup 1 PUREfrex 2.0 (GeneFrontier PF201)
    reaction [2]:

    Reagent         Stock    Volume
    ───────────────────────────────
    water                   1.50 µL
    solution I              5.00 µL
    solution II             0.50 µL
    solution III            1.00 µL
    f89           1000 nM   2.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────
                           10.00 µL

    - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
      on ice.
    - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
    - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

 5. Setup 1 NEBExpress (NEB E5360) reaction [3]:

    Reagent                    Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
    S30 extract                        2.40 µL
    synthesis buffer              2x   5.00 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine  40 U/µL   0.20 µL
    f89                      1000 nM   2.40 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
                                      10.00 µL

    - Thaw all components on ice.
    - Gently vortex the S30 extract and the synthesis
      buffer before use.
    - Add template after nuclease inhibitors.

 6. Setup 1 S30 (Promega L1030) reaction [4]:

    Reagent                        Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
    complete amino acid mixture            1.00 µL
    S30 premix                             4.00 µL
    S30 extract, linear                    3.00 µL
    f89                          1000 nM   2.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                                          10.00 µL

    - Gently mix all thawed components before use.

 7. Incubate all 4 IVTT reactions at 37°C for 30 min.

 8. Prepare 200 nM f89 as a control.

 9. Prepare 5 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                     Stock    Volume      7.5x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                               4.00 µL  30.00 µL
    Bolt LDS sample buffer         4x   2.50 µL  18.75 µL
    Bolt reducing agent           10x   1.00 µL   7.50 µL
    sample                  100 ng/µL   2.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                       10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

10. Run a gel:

    - Gel: 4−12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    - Buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    - Load 10 µL of each sample.
    - Run at 165V for 42 min.

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40
