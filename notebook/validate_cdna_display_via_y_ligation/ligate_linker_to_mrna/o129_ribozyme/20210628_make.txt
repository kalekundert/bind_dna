$ sw make f123 f124

1. Setup 2 PCR reactions [1-3]:

   Reagent           Stock    Volume      2.2x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    19.50 µL  42.90 µL
   p177,p178      20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
   o10              100 µM   0.25 µL   0.55 µL
   o242             100 µM   0.25 µL   0.55 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  25.00 µL  55.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                            50.00 µL  45.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 30s
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

3. Label the products: f121, f122

4. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
   (28204) [4,5]:

   - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
   - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
   - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
   - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
     pH=5.0.
   - Load on a MinElute column.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

   - Add 750 µL PE.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Add 10 µL EB.
   - Wait at least 1m.
   - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

5. Concentrate the samples to 500 ng/uL by
   lyophilization.

6. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
   (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

7. Setup 2 in vitro transcription reactions [6]:

   Reagent                  Stock    Volume      2.2x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
   nuclease-free water              6.00 µL  13.20 µL
   reaction buffer            10x   2.00 µL   4.40 µL
   rNTP mix                100 mM   8.00 µL  17.60 µL
   HiScribe T7                10x   2.00 µL   4.40 µL
   f121,f122            500 ng/µL   2.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   20.00 µL  18.00 µL/rxn

   - Mix reagents in the order given.
   - Ok to handle at room temperature.

8. Incubate at 37°C for 2h [7].

9. Label the products: f123, f124

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[2] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[4] https://www.qiagen.com/HB-2069

[5] Column capacity: 5 µg

[6] https://tinyurl.com/cbnz445t

[7] For reaction times longer than 60 minutes, we
    recommend using a dry air incubator or a PCR
    instrument, to prevent evaporation.
