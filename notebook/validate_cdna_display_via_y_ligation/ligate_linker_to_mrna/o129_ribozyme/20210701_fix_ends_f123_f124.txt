$ sw fix_ends_f123_f124

 1. Setup 2 phosphatase reactions:

    Reagent      Stock     Volume      2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────
    water                 7.00 µL  15.40 µL
    buffer         10x   11.00 µL  24.20 µL
    T4 PNK     10 U/µL    2.00 µL   4.40 µL
    f123,f124     4 µM   90.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────
                        110.00 µL  20.00 µL/rxn

 2. Incubate at 37°C for 6h

 3. Concentrate samples to 4 µg/µL by lyophilization:

    - f123: 19.1 µL
    - f124: 16.6 µL

 4. Prepare 2 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent            Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────
    RNA loading dye       2x  20.00 µL
    f123,f124        4 µg/µL  20.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────
                              40.00 µL

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

 5. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL low range ssRNA ladder (NEB N0364S)
    samples: 10 µL/lane
    run: 180V for 40 min

 6. Cut the 404 nt bands out of the gel [1].

    - Place the gel over a TLC plate.
    - Use a UV light to visualize the RNA (dark
      spot).
    - Consider visualizing remaining gel to ensure
      that all desired RNA was excised.

 7. Crush the gel slices.

    - Poke a hole in the bottom of a 0.65 mL tube
      with a 27 g needle.
    - Place gel slice inside the 0.65 mL tube.
    - Place the 0.65 mL tube inside a 1.5 mL tube.
    - Spin at max speed for 5 min.

 8. Resuspend gel in 400 µL PAGE elution buffer:

    Reagent               Stock      Volume
    ───────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water           878.00 µL
    Tris, pH 7.5            1 M    10.00 µL
    NaCl                    5 M   100.00 µL
    EDTA                 500 mM     2.00 µL
    SDS                     10%    10.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────
                                 1000.00 µL

 9. Incubate at 4°C overnight with end-over-end
    mixing.

10. Remove gel debris by spin filtration:

    - Load samples onto a 0.22 µm cellose-acetate
      Spin-X column [2].
    - Spin at 13,000g for 5 min.

11. Purify using Zymo Clean & Concentrator-25 kit
    (R1017) [3,4]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 16000g.
    - Add 800.0 µL RNA binding buffer to the crude
      RNA.
    - Add 1200.0 µL 95-100% ethanol to the crude RNA.
    - Load on a Zymo-Spin IICR column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 700 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 50 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

Notes:
[1] Based on Fitzy's DNA PAGE purification protocol,
    [Nilson2013], and [Petrov2013].

[2] Nylon and cellulose nitrate have high DNA-
    binding: https://tinyurl.com/3pkyc8dr

[3] https://tinyurl.com/jn3nw4c8

[4] Column capacity: 50 µg
