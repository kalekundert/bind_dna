$ sw make p177 p178

1. Setup 1 XmnI digestion [1,2]:

   Reagent               Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────────────────
   water                         5.93 µL
   p49               389 ng/µL   2.57 µL
   rCutSmart Buffer        10x   1.00 µL
   XmnI                20 U/µL   0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────
                                10.00 µL

2. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

   - 37°C for 5–15 min
   - 65°C for 20 min

3. Label the product: f120

4. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
   (28204) [4,5]:

   - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
   - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
   - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
   - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
     pH=5.0.
   - Load on a MinElute column.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

   - Add 750 µL PE.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Add 10.0 µL EB.
   - Wait at least 1m.
   - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

5. Setup 2 Gibson assemblies [6]:

   Reagent                Stock   Volume     2.2x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                         0.24 µL  0.53 µL
   Gibson master mix         2x  2.50 µL  5.50 µL
   f120               100 ng/µL  0.82 µL  1.81 µL
   f90,f91             10 ng/µL  1.44 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 5.00 µL  3.56 µL/rxn

6. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

7. Label the products: p177, p178

8. Miniprep.

Notes:
[1] https://www.neb.com/products/r0194-xmni

[2] NEB recommends 5–10 units of enzyme per µg DNA
    (10–20 units for genomic DNA).  Enzyme volume
    should not exceed 10% of the total reaction
    volume to prevent star activity due to excess
    glycerol.

[3] The heat inactivation step is not necessary if
    the DNA will be purified before use.

[4] https://www.qiagen.com/HB-2069

[5] Column capacity: 5 µg
