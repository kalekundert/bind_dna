 1. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    RNA template (f111):  −  +  −  +  −  +  −  +  −  +  −  +
    RNase A:              −  −  +  +  −  −  +  +  −  −  +  +
    Gel:                  A  A  A  A  B  B  B  B  C  C  C  C

    - A: Bolt Bis-Tris-MES 4-16%
    - B: Novex Tris-Tricine 10-20%
    - C: Novex Tris-Tricine 16%

 2. Setup 2 PUREfrex 1.0 (GeneFrontier PF001)
    reactions [1]:

    Reagent                       Stock    Volume      2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                 2.50 µL   5.50 µL
    solution I                            5.00 µL  11.00 µL
    solution II                           0.50 µL   1.10 µL
    solution III                          0.50 µL   1.10 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine     40 U/µL   0.20 µL   0.44 µL
    FluoroTect GreenLys                   0.50 µL   1.10 µL
    f111                       10000 nM   0.80 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         10.00 µL   9.20 µL/rxn

    - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
      on ice.
    - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
    - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

 3. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

 4. Split each reaction into 2x 4 µL aliquots.

 5. Add 0.4 µL RNase cocktail (Invitrogen AM2286) to
    the +RNase A aliquots.

 6. Incubate at 37°C for 15 min.

 7. Prepare 4 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume        6x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                         5.50 µL  33.00 µL
    TGLEV                    4x   2.50 µL  15.00 µL
    Bolt reducing agent     10x   1.00 µL   6.00 µL
    sample               160 nM   1.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

 8. Run a gel:

    gel: 4–12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    ladder: 5 µL SeeBlue Plus2 (Invitrogen LC5925)
    samples: 10 µL
    run: 165V for 42 min
