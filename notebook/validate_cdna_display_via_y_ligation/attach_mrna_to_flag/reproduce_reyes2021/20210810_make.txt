$ sw make f127

 1. Setup 1 annealing reaction:

    Reagent                     Stock    Volume
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
    water                              33.67 µL
    T4 DNA ligase buffer          10x  10.00 µL
    FLAG mRNA (f111)            10 µM  50.00 µL
    [Reyes2021] linker (o243)  100 µM   5.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
                                       98.67 µL

 2. Incubate as follows:

    - 90°C for 30s
    - Cool to 25°C at 1°C/s

 3. Add to the reaction:

    - 0.33 µL × 10 U/µL = 3U T4 PNK
    - 2 µL × 10 U/µL = 20U T4 RNA ligase

 4. Incubate at 25°C for 30 min.

 5. Label the product: f127

 6. Concentrate the sample to 5 uL by lyophilization.

 7. Prepare 1 sample for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent          Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────
    RNA loading dye     2x   5.00 µL
    f127              5 uM   5.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────
                            10.00 µL

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

 8. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL low range ssRNA ladder (NEB N0364S)
    samples: 10 µL/lane
    run: 180V for 40 min

 9. Cut the desired band out of the gel [1].

    - Place the gel over a TLC plate.
    - Use a UV light to visualize the RNA (dark
      spot).
    - Consider visualizing remaining gel to ensure
      that all desired RNA was excised.

10. Crush the gel slice.

    - Poke a hole in the bottom of a 0.65 mL tube
      with a 27 g needle.
    - Place gel slice inside the 0.65 mL tube.
    - Place the 0.65 mL tube inside a 1.5 mL tube.
    - Spin at max speed for 5 min.

11. Resuspend gel in 400 µL PAGE elution buffer:

    Reagent               Stock     Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          439.00 µL
    Tris, pH 7.5            1 M    5.00 µL
    NaCl                    5 M   50.00 µL
    EDTA                 500 mM    1.00 µL
    SDS                     10%    5.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────
                                 500.00 µL

12. Incubate at 4°C overnight with end-over-end
    mixing.

13. Remove gel debris by spin filtration:

    - Load samples onto a 0.22 µm cellose-acetate
      Spin-X column [2].
    - Spin at 13,000g for 5 min.

14. Purify using Zymo Clean & Concentrator-5 kit
    (R1015) [3,4]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 16000g.
    - Add 800 µL RNA binding buffer to the crude RNA.
    - Add 1200 µL 95-100% ethanol to the crude RNA.
    - Load on a Zymo-Spin IICR column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 700 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 15 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

15. Make 5 µL, 2 µM aliquots.

Notes:
[1] Based on Fitzy's DNA PAGE purification protocol,
    [Nilson2013], and [Petrov2013].

[2] Nylon and cellulose nitrate have high DNA-
    binding: https://tinyurl.com/3pkyc8dr

[3] https://tinyurl.com/yd699u8b

[4] Column capacity: 10 µg
