1. Prepare each 10x primer mix:

   Reagent    Conc  Each Rxn
   ─────────────────────────
   water            18.00 μL
   87       100 μM   1.00 μL
   88       100 μM   1.00 μL
   ─────────────────────────
                    20.00 μL

2. Setup 15 PCR reactions and 1 negative control:

   Reagent               Conc  Each Rxn  Master Mix
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                        6.00 μL    96.00 μL
   primer mix             10x   2.00 μL    32.00 μL
   digest           100 pg/µL   2.00 µL            
   SsoAdvanced mix         2x  10.00 µL   160.00 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
                               20.00 μL    18.00 μL/rxn

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 95°C for 30s
   - Repeat 40x:
     - 95°C for 10s
     - 65°C for 15s
   - 65-95°C in 5s steps of 0.5°C
