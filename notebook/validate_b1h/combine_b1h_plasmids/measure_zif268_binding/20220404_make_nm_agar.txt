$ sw b1h/make_nm_agar 10

1. Prepare ≈586 mL 12/11x NM-agar [1]:

   Reagent                      Final      Quantity
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
   M9 salts                     1x           6.72 g
   −His/−Ura DO supplement [2]            480.00 mg
   water                               to 559.36 mL

   - Add 9.6 g agar
   - Autoclave at 121°C for 45 min.
   - Cool to ≈55°C [3].

   Reagent            Stock          Final     Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
   M9-agar                                  559.36 mL
   glucose        200 mg/mL        4 mg/mL   12.80 mL
   thiamine       200 mg/mL        4 mg/mL   12.80 mL
   MgSO₄             100 mM           1 mM    6.40 mL
   ZnSO₄             100 mM          10 µM   64.00 µL
   CaCl₂                1 M         100 µM   64.00 µL
   carbenicillin  100 mg/mL  100 µg/mL, 1x  640.00 µL
   IPTG                 1 M          10 µM    6.40 µL

2. Prepare 265 mL permissive media:

   Reagent     Stock   Final     Volume
   ────────────────────────────────────
   NM-agar    12/11x      1x  242.92 mL
   histidine  100 mM    6 mM   15.90 mL
   uracil      20 mM  0.2 mM    2.65 mL
   water                        3.53 mL
   ────────────────────────────────────
                              265.00 mL

3. Prepare 265 mL selective media with 10 mM 3-AT:

   Reagent    Stock  Final     Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────
   NM-agar   12/11x     1x  242.92 mL
   3-AT     1000 mM  10 mM    2.65 mL
   water                     19.43 mL
   ──────────────────────────────────
                            265.00 mL

4. Pour 25 mL plates.

Notes:
[1] Noyes et al. (2008) DOI: 10.1093/nar/gkn048

[2] This supplement isn't exactly what [Noyes2008]
    calls for: it's missing 9 of the amino acids.

[3] 3-AT is heat labile and will be destroyed if
    added to media hotter that 55°C.  Store plates
    containing 3-AT at 4°C for up to 2 months.
