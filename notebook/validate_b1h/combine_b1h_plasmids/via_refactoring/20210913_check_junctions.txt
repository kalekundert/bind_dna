$ sw check_junctions

1. Pick 5 colonies and resuspend each in 20 µL water.

2. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent                                                                 Stock    Volume
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                                                           9.00 µL
   o2                                                                     100 µM   0.50 µL
   o88,o185,o188,o88,o185,o188,o88,o185,o188,o88,o185,o188,o88,o185,o188  100 µM   0.50 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                                                  10.00 µL

3. Setup 15 PCR reactions [2]:

   Reagent        Stock   Volume       17x
   ───────────────────────────────────────
   water                 1.50 µL  25.50 µL
   p170 colony           0.50 µL
   primer mix       10x  0.50 µL
   Q5 master mix     2x  2.50 µL  42.50 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────
                         5.00 µL   4.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

4. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 2 min
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

5. Prepare 15 samples for electrophoresis:

   Reagent         Stock   Volume
   ──────────────────────────────
   loading buffer     6x  1.00 µL
   sample                 5.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────
                          6.00 µL

6. Run a gel:

   gel: 1% agarose/TBE with 1x GelGreen
   buffer: 1x TBE
   samples: 5 µL/lane
   run: 100V for 60 min

7. Image with a 470 nm blue-light transilluminator.

8. Check for bands of the following sizes:

   o88: 2.1 kb
   o185: 2.2 kb
   o188: 3.2 kb

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol
