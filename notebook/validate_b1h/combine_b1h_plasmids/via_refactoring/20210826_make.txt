$ sw make p172

 1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent                              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                        9.00 µL
    o105,o109,o116,o107,o115,o113,o111  100 µM   0.50 µL
    o106,o110,o117,o108,o118,o114,o112  100 µM   0.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                10.00 µL

 2. Setup 7 PCR reactions [2,3]:

    Reagent                         Stock    Volume      7.7x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                   4.50 µL  34.65 µL
    p96,f81,f33,p166,f33,p96,p4  20 pg/µL   1.50 µL
    primer mix                        10x   1.50 µL
    Q5 master mix                      2x   7.50 µL  57.75 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                           15.00 µL  12.00 µL/rxn

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

 3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 60°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 1 min
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 4. Label the products: f35, f83, f38, f82, f36, f41,
    f40

 5. Run an E-gel of f83 to make sure the PCR worked
    well

 6. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
    (28204) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
    - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
      pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10.0 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 7. Setup 1 Golden Gate assembly [6]:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    water                        2.18 µL
    T4 ligase buffer       10x   1.00 µL
    T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL   0.25 µL
    BsaI-HFv2          20 U/µL   0.25 µL
    f35               60 ng/µL   0.78 µL
    f36               60 ng/µL   0.20 µL
    f82               60 ng/µL   1.17 µL
    f38               60 ng/µL   0.31 µL
    f83               60 ng/µL   2.45 µL
    f40               60 ng/µL   1.24 µL
    f41               60 ng/µL   0.17 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                10.00 µL

 8. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - Repeat 30 times:
    - - 37°C for 1 min
      - 16°C for 1 min
    - 60°C for 5 min

 9. Label the product: p170

10. Miniprep.

11. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    o192     100 µM   0.50 µL
    o193     100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

12. Setup 1 PCR reaction [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock   Volume
    ────────────────────────────────
    water                    1.50 µL
    p170           20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
    primer mix          10x  0.50 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL

13. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 60°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 3 min
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

14. Setup 1 ligation reaction:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    water                        6.75 µL
    T4 ligase buffer       10x   1.00 µL
    T4 PNK             10 U/µL   0.25 µL
    T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL   0.25 µL
    DpnI               20 U/µL   0.25 µL
    PCR product       50 ng/µL   1.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                10.00 µL

15. Incubate at room temperature for 1h.

16. Label the product: p171

17. Miniprep.

18. Setup 1 restriction digestion [7,8]:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    water                         3.00 µL
    p171              200 ng/µL   5.00 µL
    rCutSmart Buffer        10x   1.00 µL
    EcoRI-HF            20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    NotI-HF             20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL

19. Incubate at the following temperatures [9]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 65°C for 20 min

20. Label the product: f84

21. Setup 1 ligation reaction [10]:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    water                        0.20 µL
    T4 ligase buffer        10x  0.50 µL
    T4 DNA ligase      400 U/µL  0.25 µL
    f84               100 ng/µL  2.05 µL
    f80                   90 nM  2.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                 5.00 µL

22. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 15 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

23. Label the product: p172

24. Miniprep.
