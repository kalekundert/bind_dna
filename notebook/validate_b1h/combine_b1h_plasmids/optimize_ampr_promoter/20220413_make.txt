$ sw make p216 p217 p219 p220

 1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    o359     100 µM   0.50 µL
    sr71     100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

 2. Setup 1 PCR reaction [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────
    water                     6.00 µL
    p186           20 pg/µL   2.00 µL
    primer mix          10x   2.00 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  10.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────
                             20.00 µL

 3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 63°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 4 min
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 4. Label the product: f169

 5. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 6. Setup 4 Gibson assemblies [6]:

    Reagent                 Stock   Volume      4.4x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────
    Gibson master mix          2x  2.50 µL  11.00 µL
    f169                 80 ng/µL  1.47 µL   6.49 µL
    f172,f173,f175,f176  10 ng/µL  1.03 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   5.00 µL   3.97 µL/rxn

 7. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

 8. Label the products: p216, p217, p219, p220

 9. Transform the following plasmids: p216, p217,
    p219, p220 [7]

    - Pre-warm 4 LB+Carb plates.
    - For each transformation:

      - Thaw 100 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
      - Add 5 µL plasmid.
      - Gently flick to mix

      - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
      - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
      - Add 100 µL SOC.

      - Plate 200 µL cells.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

10. Sequence the following plasmids:

    Plasmid  Primers
    ────────────────
    p216     o266
    p217     o266
    p219     o266
    p220     o266

11. Miniprep.

Notes:

    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
