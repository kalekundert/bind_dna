$ sw make p216 p217 p220

1. Setup 3 Gibson assemblies [1]:

   Reagent               Stock   Volume     3.3x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────
   Gibson master mix        2x  2.50 µL  8.25 µL
   f169               65 ng/uL  1.60 µL  5.27 µL
   f172,f173,f176     10 ng/µL  0.90 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL  4.10 µL/rxn

2. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

3. Label the products: p216, p217, p220

4. Transform the following plasmids: p216, p217, p220
   [2]

   - Pre-warm 3 LB+Carb plates.
   - For each transformation:

     - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
     - Add 1 µL plasmid.
     - Gently flick to mix

     - Plate 25 µL cells.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

5. Sequence the following plasmids:

   Plasmid  Primers
   ────────────────
   p216     o266
   p217     o266
   p220     o266

6. Miniprep.
