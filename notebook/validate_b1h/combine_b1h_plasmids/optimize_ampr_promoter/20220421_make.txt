$ sw make p216

1. Setup 1 Gibson assembly [1]:

   Reagent               Stock   Volume
   ────────────────────────────────────
   Gibson master mix        2x  2.50 µL
   f169               65 ng/uL  1.74 µL
   f172               10 ng/µL  0.76 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL

2. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

3. Label the product: p216

4. Transform p216 into MACH1 [2]:

   - Pre-warm 1 LB+Carb plate.
   - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
   - Add 1 µL plasmid.
   - Gently flick to mix

   - Plate 25 µL cells.
   - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

5. Sequence p216 with o266.

6. Miniprep.
