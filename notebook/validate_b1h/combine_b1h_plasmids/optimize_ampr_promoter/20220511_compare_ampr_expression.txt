1. Grow overnight cultures:

   LB+Carb: s13,s16,s23,s24,s25,s26,s27

   - Use fresh colonies (i.e. either transformed or
     restreaked within 1 week).
   - Incubate at 37°C overnight with shaking.

2. Grow day cultures:

   - Inoculate 3 mL LB+Carb with 30 µL saturated
     overnight culture.
   - Incubate at 37°C with shaking until the cultures
     reach log phase, ≈4h.

3. Prepare 5 mL 8/7x permissive media:

   Reagent   Stock        Final     Volume
   ───────────────────────────────────────
   NM                              4.60 mL
   His      100 mM   6.85714 mM  342.86 µL
   Ura       20 mM  0.228571 mM   57.14 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────
                                   5.00 mL

4. Prepare 5 mL 8/7x selective media with 10 mM 3-AT:

   Reagent    Stock       Final    Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────────
   NM                             4.94 mL
   3-AT     1000 mM  11.4286 mM  57.14 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────
                                  5.00 mL

5. Wash 1 mL cells with minimal media:

   - Repeat 4x:
     - Spin 3500g, 3 min, 4°C.
     - Resuspend in 1 mL NM.

6. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

   - Put 146.25 µL 0.1 OD cells in a tube.
   - Put 100.00 µL NM in 2 adjacent tubes.
   - Transfer 46.25 µL between each tube to make 3
     3.2-fold dilutions.

7. Setup a clear-bottom 96-well plate as follows:

   - 175 µL 8/7x media
   - 25 µL cell dilution
   - Fill all remaining wells with 200 µL water.
   - Top each well with 30 µL mineral oil.

8. Weigh the plate before and after the following
   growth step, to quantify evaporation.

9. Measure cell growth for 36h using a plate reader

   - Incubate at 37°C with shaking.
   - Measure A600 every 30 min.

Note:
[1] The final concentrations will be:
    1  1.000000e-01 OD
    2  3.162278e-02 OD
    3  1.000000e-02 OD
