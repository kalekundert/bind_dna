1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent          Stock    Volume
   ────────────────────────────────
   water                    9.00 µL
   o349,o350,o343  100 µM   0.50 µL
   o344,o344,o351  100 µM   0.50 µL
   ────────────────────────────────
                           10.00 µL

2. Setup 3 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent           Stock    Volume      3.3x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    16.50 µL  54.45 µL
   p194           20 pg/µL   5.50 µL  18.15 µL
   primer mix          10x   5.50 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  27.50 µL  90.75 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                            55.00 µL  49.50 µL/rxn

   - Split into 10 identical 5 µL reactions.
   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 56-71°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 3m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Run a 1% E-gel.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
