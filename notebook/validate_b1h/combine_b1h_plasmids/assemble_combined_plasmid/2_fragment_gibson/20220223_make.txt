$ sw make p184

1. Setup 1 Gibson assembly [1]:

   Reagent               Stock   Volume
   ────────────────────────────────────
   Gibson master mix        2x  2.50 µL
   f147               27 ng/uL  1.33 µL
   f154               13 ng/uL  1.17 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL

2. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

3. Label the product: p184

4. Transform p184 into MACH1 [2]:

   - Pre-warm 1 LB+Amp plate.
   - Thaw 10 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
   - Add 2 µL plasmid.
   - Gently flick to mix

   - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
   - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
   - Add 90 µL SOC.

   - Plate 100 µL cells.
   - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

5. Miniprep.
