 1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent          Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────
    water                    9.00 µL
    o289,o291,o293  100 µM   0.50 µL
    o290,o292,o294  100 µM   0.50 µL

                            10.00 µL

 2. Setup 3 PCR reactions [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume     ≈3.7x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
    water                     4.50 µL  16.50 µL
    f33,p166,f33   20 pg/µL   1.50 µL
    primer mix          10x   1.50 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x   7.50 µL  27.50 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
                             15.00 µL  12.00 µL/rxn

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

 3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 65°C,61°C,63°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 30s
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 4. Label the products: f151, f152, f153

 5. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10.0 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

14. Prepare 3 samples for electrophoresis:

    Reagent         Stock   Volume
    ──────────────────────────────
    loading buffer     6x  1.00 µL
    sample                 5.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────
                           6.00 µL

15. Run a gel:

    gel: 1% agarose/TBE with 1x GelGreen
    buffer: 1x TBE
    samples: 5 µL/lane
    run: 100V for 60 min

16. Image with a 470 nm blue-light transilluminator.

17. Cut desired band out of an agarose gel.

18. Place the gel slice in a Spin-X column with
    1 µL/mg EB.  Spin at 13,000g for 5-20 min
    at room temperature [6].
    
    The following optional steps may improve yield:
    
    - Macerate the gel.
    - Freeze/thaw the gel.
    - Add 200 µL EB after the first spin and
      re-centrifuge.
    - Spin for longer.

19. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10.0 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

20. Setup 6 Gibson assemblies [7]:

22. Label the products: p184, p185, p186, p187, p188,
    p189
