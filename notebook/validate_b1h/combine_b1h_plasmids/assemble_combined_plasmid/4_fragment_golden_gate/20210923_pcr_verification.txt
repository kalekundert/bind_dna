$ sw pcr_verification

1. Resuspend each colony in 20 µL EB.

2. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent                                        Stock    Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                                  9.00 µL
   o2                                            100 µM   0.50 µL
   o262,o185,o188,o262,o185,o188,o262,o185,o188  100 µM   0.50 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                         10.00 µL

3. Setup 9 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent                                          Stock   Volume       11x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                                   1.50 µL  16.50 µL
   p184,p184,p184,p185,p185,p185,p186,p186,p186  20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
   primer mix                                         10x  0.50 µL
   Q5 master mix                                       2x  2.50 µL  27.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                           5.00 µL   4.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

4. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 61°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 2 min
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

5. Run a 1% E-gel.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
