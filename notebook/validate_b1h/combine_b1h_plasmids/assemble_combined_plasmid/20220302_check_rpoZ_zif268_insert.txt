1. Resuspend each colony in 20 µL EB.

2. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent   Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────
   water             9.00 µL
   o2       100 µM   0.50 µL
   o266     100 µM   0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────
                    10.00 µL

3. Setup 8 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent           Stock   Volume       10x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    1.50 µL  15.00 µL
   8×p184         20 pg/µL  0.50 µL   5.00 µL
   primer mix          10x  0.50 µL   5.00 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL  25.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
                            5.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

4. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 61°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

5. Run a 1% E-gel EX.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
