$ sw make f35 f83

1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent     Stock    Volume
   ───────────────────────────
   water               9.00 µL
   o105,o109  100 µM   0.50 µL
   o106,o110  100 µM   0.50 µL
   ───────────────────────────
                      10.00 µL

2. Setup 2 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent           Stock    Volume      2.2x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    15.00 µL  33.00 µL
   p96,f81        20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
   primer mix          10x   5.00 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  25.00 µL  55.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                            50.00 µL  40.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1 min
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Label the products: f35, f83

5. Cut desired band out of an agarose gel.

6. Place the gel slice in a Spin-X column with
   100-200 µL EB.  Spin at 13,000g for 5-20 min
   at room temperature [4].
   
   The following optional steps may improve yield:
   
   - Macerate the gel.
   - Freeze/thaw the gel.
   - Add 200 µL EB after the first spin and
     re-centrifuge.
   - Spin for longer.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[4] https://tinyurl.com/fh2jtd
