September 1, 2021

$ sw make p172

 1. Setup 1 Golden Gate assembly [1]:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    T4 ligase buffer        10x   1.00 µL
    T4 DNA ligase      400 U/µL   0.25 µL
    BsaI-HFv2           20 U/µL   0.25 µL
    f35               104 ng/uL   0.22 µL
    f36                67 ng/uL   0.09 µL
    f82               124 ng/uL   0.28 µL
    f38                80 ng/uL   0.12 µL
    f83                10 ng/uL   7.35 µL
    f40               129 ng/uL   0.29 µL
    f41                36 ng/uL   0.14 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL

 2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - Repeat 30 times:
    - - 37°C for 1 min
      - 16°C for 1 min
    - 60°C for 5 min

 3. Label the product: p170

 4. Miniprep.

 5. Prepare 10x primer mix [2]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    o192     100 µM   0.50 µL
    o193     100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

 6. Setup 1 PCR reaction [3,4]:

    Reagent           Stock   Volume
    ────────────────────────────────
    water                    1.50 µL
    p170           20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
    primer mix          10x  0.50 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL

 7. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 60°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 3 min
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 8. Setup 1 ligation reaction:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    water                        6.75 µL
    T4 ligase buffer       10x   1.00 µL
    T4 PNK             10 U/µL   0.25 µL
    T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL   0.25 µL
    DpnI               20 U/µL   0.25 µL
    PCR product       50 ng/µL   1.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                10.00 µL

 9. Incubate at room temperature for 1h.

10. Label the product: p171

11. Miniprep.

12. Setup 1 restriction digestion [5,6]:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    water                         3.00 µL
    p171              200 ng/µL   5.00 µL
    rCutSmart Buffer        10x   1.00 µL
    EcoRI-HF            20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    NotI-HF             20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL

13. Incubate at the following temperatures [7]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 65°C for 20 min

14. Label the product: f84

15. Setup 1 ligation reaction [8]:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    water                        0.20 µL
    T4 ligase buffer        10x  0.50 µL
    T4 DNA ligase      400 U/µL  0.25 µL
    f84               100 ng/µL  2.05 µL
    f80                   90 nM  2.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                 5.00 µL

16. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 15 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

17. Label the product: p172

18. Miniprep.
