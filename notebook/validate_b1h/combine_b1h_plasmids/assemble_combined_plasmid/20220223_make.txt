$ sw make p194 p195 p196

1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent   Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────
   water             9.00 µL
   o304     100 µM   0.50 µL
   o305     100 µM   0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────
                    10.00 µL

2. Setup 3 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent            Stock   Volume        5x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                     1.50 µL   7.50 µL
   p185,p187,p188  20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
   primer mix           10x  0.50 µL   2.50 µL
   Q5 master mix         2x  2.50 µL  12.50 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL   4.50 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 59°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 4 min
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Setup 3 ligation reactions:

   Reagent              Stock    Volume     ≈3.4x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                        6.75 µL  23.29 µL
   T4 ligase buffer       10x   1.00 µL   3.45 µL
   T4 PNK             10 U/µL   0.25 µL   0.86 µL
   T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL   0.25 µL   0.86 µL
   DpnI               20 U/µL   0.25 µL   0.86 µL
   PCR product       50 ng/µL   1.50 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                               10.00 µL   8.50 µL/rxn

5. Incubate at room temperature for 1h.

6. Label the products: p194, p195, p196

7. Transform the following plasmids: p194, p195, p196
   [4]

   - Pre-warm 3 LB+Amp plates.
   - For each transformation:

     - Thaw 10 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
     - Add 2 µL plasmid.
     - Gently flick to mix

     - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
     - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
     - Add 90 µL SOC.

     - Plate 100 µL cells.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

8. Sequence the following plasmids:

   Plasmid  Primers
   ────────────────
   p194     o2
   p195     o2
   p196     o2

9. Miniprep.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
