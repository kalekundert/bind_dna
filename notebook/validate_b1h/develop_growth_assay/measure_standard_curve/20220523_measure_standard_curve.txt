 1. Grow overnight cultures:

    LB+Carb: s16,s22,sz213,sz214,sz215
    LB+Carb+Kan: s4,s5,s36,s37,s38

    - Use fresh colonies (i.e. either transformed or
      restreaked within 1 week).
    - Incubate at 37°C overnight with shaking.

 2. Grow day cultures:

    - Inoculate 3 mL LB+Carb/LB+Carb+Kan with 30 µL
      saturated overnight culture.
    - Incubate at 37°C with shaking until the
      cultures reach log phase, ≈4h.

 7. Wash 1 mL cells with minimal media:

    - Repeat 4x:
      - Spin 3500g, 3 min, 4°C.
      - Resuspend in 1 mL NM.

 8. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 146.25 µL 0.1 OD cells in a tube.
    - Put 100.00 µL NM in 2 adjacent tubes.
    - Transfer 46.25 µL between each tube to make 3
      3.2-fold dilutions.

 9. Setup a clear-bottom 96-well plate as follows:

    - 175 µL 8/7x media
    - 25 µL cell dilution
    - Fill all remaining wells with 200 µL water.
    - Top each well with 30 µL mineral oil.

10. Weigh the plate before and after the following
    growth step, to quantify evaporation.

11. Measure cell growth for 36h using a plate reader:

    - Incubate at 37°C with shaking.
    - Measure A600 every 30 min.
