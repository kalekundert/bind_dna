$ sw transform

1. Transform the following plasmids: p166+p167,
   p166+p168, p166+p210, p166+p211, p166+p212, p189,
   p202, p213, p214, p215 [1]

   - Pre-warm selective plates:
     LB+Amp+Kan: p166+p167, p166+p168, p166+p210,
       p166+p211, p166+p212
     LB+Amp: p189, p202, p213, p214, p215
   - For each transformation:

     - Thaw 100 µL competent s3 cells on ice.
     - Add 2 µL plasmid.
     - Gently flick to mix.
     - Incubate on ice for 30 min.

     - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
     - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
     - Add 1000 µL SOC.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 60 min with end-over-end
       mixing.

     - Spin at 5000g for 5 min.
     - Remove 900 µL media.
     - Resuspend pelleted cells.

     - Plate 200 µL cells.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

2. Label the products: s4, s5, s36, s37, s38, s16,
   s22, sz213, sz214, sz215
