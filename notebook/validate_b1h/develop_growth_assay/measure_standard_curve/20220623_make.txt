$ sw make s36

1. Transform p166+p210 into s3 [1]:

   - Pre-warm 1 LB+Amp+Kan plate.
   - Thaw 100 µL competent s3 cells on ice.
   - Add 2 µL plasmid.
   - Gently flick to mix.
   - Incubate on ice for 30 min.

   - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
   - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
   - Add 1000 µL SOC.
   - Incubate at 37°C for 60 min with end-over-end
     mixing.

   - Spin at 5000g for 5 min.
   - Remove 900 µL media.
   - Resuspend pelleted cells.

   - Plate 200 µL cells.
   - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

2. Label the product: s36
