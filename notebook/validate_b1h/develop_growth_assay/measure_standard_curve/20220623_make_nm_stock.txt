$ sw b1h/make_nm_stock

1. Prepare 500 mL 2x NM −His/−Ura [1]:
   
   Reagent                          Stock       Final     Amount
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                               to 500 mL
   M9 salts                                      2×1x     10.5 g
   −His/−Ura DO supplement [2]                   2×1x     750 mg
   glucose                      200 mg/mL   2×4 mg/mL      20 mL
   thiamine                       1 mg/mL  2×10 µg/mL      10 mL 
   MgSO₄                           100 mM      2×1 mM      10 mL 
   ZnSO₄                           100 mM     2×10 µM     100 µL 
   CaCl₂                               1M    2×100 µM     100 µL 
   
   - Sterilize using a 0.22 µm filter.

2. Before using, add the following to the 1x media 
   (in addition to His, Ura, 3-AT, etc.):
   
   Reagent            Stock            Final      Amount
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   IPTG                  1M            10 µM  0.01 µL/mL
   kanamycin       50 mg/mL   25 µg/mL, 0.5x   0.5 µL/mL
   carbenicillin  100 mg/mL  100 µg/mL,   1x     1 µL/mL

Notes:
[1] Noyes et al. (2008) DOI: 10.1093/nar/gkn048

[2] Clontech 630422.  This supplement isn't 
    exactly what [Noyes2008] calls for: it's 
    missing 9 of the amino acids.
