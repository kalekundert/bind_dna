$ sw make p210 p211 p212 p213 p214 p215

 1. Setup 3 annealing reactions:

    Reagent          Stock   Volume
    ───────────────────────────────
    PBS                10x  0.40 µL
    o352,o354,o356  100 µM  1.80 µL
    o353,o355,o357  100 µM  1.80 µL
    ───────────────────────────────
                            4.00 µL

 2. Perform the annealing reactions:

    - Incubate at 95°C for 2 min.
    - Cool at room temperature.

 3. Label the products: f165, f166, f167

 4. Dilute f165, f166, and f167 to 90 nM.

 5. Setup 2 restriction digestions [1,2]:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume     2.2x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    rCutSmart Buffer       10x   2.33 µL  5.13 µL
    BsrGI-HF           20 U/µL   0.50 µL  1.10 µL
    EcoRI-HF           20 U/µL   0.50 µL  1.10 µL
    p189,p168         50 ng/µL  20.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                23.33 µL  3.33 µL/rxn

 6. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 65°C for 20 min
    - 80°C for 20 min

 7. Label the products: f163, f168

 8. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 9. Setup 6 ligation reactions [6]:

    Reagent                           Stock   Volume     6.6x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    T4 ligase buffer                    10x  0.50 µL  3.30 µL
    T4 DNA ligase                  400 U/µL  0.25 µL  1.65 µL
    T4 PNK                          10 U/µL  0.25 µL  1.65 µL
    f168,f168,f168,f163,f163,f163  80 ng/µL  2.45 µL
    f165,f166,f167,f165,f166,f167     90 nM  1.55 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                             5.00 µL  1.00 µL/rxn

10. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 15 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

11. Label the products: p210, p211, p212, p213, p214,
    p215

12. Transform the following plasmids: p210, p211,
    p212, p213, p214, p215 [7]

    - Pre-warm selective plates:
      LB+Kan: p210, p211, p212
      LB+Amp: p213, p214, p215
    - For each transformation:

      - Thaw 100 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
      - Add 5 µL plasmid.
      - Gently flick to mix
      - Incubate on ice for 30 min.

      - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
      - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
      - Add 100 µL SOC.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 60 min with end-over-end
        mixing.

      - Plate 200 µL cells.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

13. Sequence the following plasmids:

    Plasmid  Primers
    ────────────────
    p210     o251
    p211     o251
    p212     o251
    p213     o251
    p214     o251
    p215     o251

14. Miniprep.
