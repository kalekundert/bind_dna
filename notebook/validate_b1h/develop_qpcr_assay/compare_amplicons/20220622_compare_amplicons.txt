 1. Grow overnight cultures:

    LB+Carb: sz224,sz228

    - Inoculate 1 mL sterile media.
    - Incubate overnight at 37°C with shaking at 225
      rpm.

 2. Grow day cultures:

    LB + 10 µM IPTG + Carb: sz224,sz228

    - Inoculate 1 mL sterile media with 10 µL
      saturated overnight culture.
    - Incubate at 37°C with shaking at 225 rpm for
      4h.

 3. Extract total RNA using the Zymo Quick-RNA
    miniprep kit.

 4. Prepare enough reverse transcriptase (RT) master
    mix for 4 reactions:

    Reagent                      Stock    Volume      4.4x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    DEPC-treated water                  11.00 µL  48.40 µL
    SuperScript VILO master mix     5x   4.00 µL  17.60 µL

 5. Denature half of the RT master mix:

    - Take 33.00 µL of the above mix.
    - Incubate at 65°C for 10m.

 6. Setup 4 reverse transcription reactions:

    - Use all combinations of the following reagents:

      RT master mix: denatured, non-denatured
      template RNA: sz224, sz228

    - Mix the reactions:

      Reagent            Stock    Volume
      ──────────────────────────────────
      RT master mix             15.00 µL
      template RNA   500 ng/µL   5.00 µL
      ──────────────────────────────────
                                20.00 µL

 7. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 25°C for 10m
    - 42°C for 60m
    - 85°C for 5m

 8. Store any cDNA not used for qPCR at −20°C.

 9. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent                       Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                 9.00 µL
    sr1,sr3,o366,o368,o370,o372  100 µM   0.50 µL
    sr2,sr5,o367,o369,o371,o373  100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         10.00 µL

10. Setup 24 qPCR reactions [2]:

    Reagent                              Stock    Volume      26.4x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                       15.40 µL  406.56 µL
    sz224+RT,sz224−RT,sz228+RT,sz228−RT          2.20 µL
    primer mix                             10x   4.40 µL
    SsoAdvanced supermix                    2x  22.00 µL  580.80 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                44.00 µL   37.40 µL/rxn

    - Split into 2 identical 20 µL reactions.
    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

11. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 95°C for 30s
    - Repeat 40x:
      - 95°C for 10s
      - 61°C,57°C,59°C for 15s
      - Measure fluorescence
    - 65.0-95.0°C in 5s steps of 0.5°C:
      - Measure fluorescence

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol
