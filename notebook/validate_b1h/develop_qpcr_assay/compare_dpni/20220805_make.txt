$ sw make p232 p233

1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent   Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────
   water             9.00 µL
   o374     100 µM   0.50 µL
   o375     100 µM   0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────
                    10.00 µL

2. Setup 2 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent           Stock   Volume        4x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    1.50 µL   6.00 µL
   p224,p228      20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
   primer mix          10x  0.50 µL   2.00 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL  10.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
                            5.00 µL   4.50 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 62°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 3m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Setup 2 ligation reactions:

   Reagent                 Stock    Volume      2.3x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                           3.00 µL   6.90 µL
   KLD reaction buffer        2x   5.00 µL  11.50 µL
   KLD enzyme mix            10x   1.00 µL   2.30 µL
   PCR product          50 ng/µL   1.00 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                  10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

5. Incubate at room temperature for 5m.

6. Label the products: p232, p233

7. Transform the following plasmids: p232, p233 [4]

   - Pre-warm 2 LB+Amp plates.
   - For each transformation:

     - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
     - Add 1 µL plasmid.
     - Gently flick to mix.

     - Plate 25 µL cells.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

8. Sequence the following plasmids:

   Plasmid  Primers
   ────────────────
   p232     sr5
   p233     sr5

9. Miniprep.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[4] https://tinyurl.com/2cesd2hv
