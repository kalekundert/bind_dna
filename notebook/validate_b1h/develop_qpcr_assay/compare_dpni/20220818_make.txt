$ sw make sz234 sz235

1. Transform the following plasmids: p234, p235 [1]

   - Pre-warm 2 LB+Amp plates.
   - For each transformation:

     - Thaw 100 µL competent s3 cells on ice.
     - Add 2 µL plasmid.
     - Gently flick to mix.

     - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
     - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
     - Plate 200 µL cells.
     - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

2. Label the products: sz234, sz235
