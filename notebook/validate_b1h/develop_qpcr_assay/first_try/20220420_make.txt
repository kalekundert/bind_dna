$ sw make p221 p222 p223 p224

 1. Order f177 from IDT.

 2. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    sr27     100 µM   0.50 µL
    sr31     100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

 3. Setup 1 PCR reaction [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────
    water                    15.00 µL
    f177           20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
    primer mix          10x   5.00 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  25.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────
                             50.00 µL

 4. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 63°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 1m30
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 5. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 6. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    water                         9.00 µL
    o360,o360,o363,o365  100 µM   0.50 µL
    o361,o362,o364,o364  100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL

 7. Setup 4 PCR reactions [2,3]:

    Reagent                 Stock    Volume       4.4x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                          15.00 µL   66.00 µL
    p194,p196,p186,p189  20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
    primer mix                10x   5.00 µL
    Q5 master mix              2x  25.00 µL  110.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   50.00 µL   40.00 µL/rxn

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

 8. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 123°C,125°C,123°C,123°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 2m30
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 9. Label the products: f178, f179, f180, f181

10. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

11. Setup 4 Gibson assemblies [6]:

    Reagent                  Stock   Volume      4.4x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                           0.89 µL   3.92 µL
    Gibson master mix           2x  2.50 µL  11.00 µL
    f178,f179,f180,f181  200 ng/µL  0.81 µL
    f177                 200 ng/µL  0.80 µL   3.53 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    5.00 µL   4.19 µL/rxn

12. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

13. Label the products: p221, p222, p223, p224

14. Transform the following plasmids: p221, p222,
    p223, p224 [7]

    - Pre-warm 4 LB+Amp plates.
    - For each transformation:

      - Thaw 100 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
      - Add 5 µL plasmid.
      - Gently flick to mix

      - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
      - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
      - Add 100 µL SOC.

      - Plate 200 µL cells.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

15. Miniprep.
