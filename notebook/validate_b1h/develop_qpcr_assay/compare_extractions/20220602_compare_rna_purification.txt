 1. Grow overnight cultures:

    LB+Carb: sz224,sz228

    - Inoculate 1 mL sterile media.
    - Incubate overnight at 37°C with shaking at 225
      rpm.

 2. Grow day cultures:

    - Inoculate 6 mL sterile media with 60 µL
      saturated overnight culture.
    - Incubate at 37°C with shaking at 225 rpm for
      4h.

 3. Purify each sample using TRIzol, Direct-zol
    (+/−DNase), and Quick-RNA miniprep (+/−DNase),
    according to manufacturers' instructions.

 4. Lyse and homogenize samples in TRIzol reagent
    [1]:

    - Collect cells by centrifugation, discard
      supernatant.
    - Add 750 µL TRIzol reagent per 250 µL sample.
    - Pipet the lysate up and down several times to
      homogenize.
    - Incubate for 5 min at room temperature.

 5. Extract RNA from TRIzol reagent [1]:

    - Add 200 µL chloroform per 1 mL TRIzol reagent.
    - Vortex vigorously.
    - Incubate for 2-3 min at room temperature.
    - Centrifuge for 15 min at 12000g and 4°C.
    - Transfer the aqueous phase (top, not pink, ≈500
      µL per 1 mL TRIzol reagent) for each sample to
      a clean tube, taking care to avoid transferring
      any of the organic phase.

 6. Concentrate and purify the RNA by isopropanol
    precipitation [1]:

    - Add 5-10 µg RNAse-free glycogen.
    - Add 500 µL isopropanol per 1 mL TRIzol reagent.
    - Incubate at 4°C for 10 min.

    - Centrifuge for 10 min at 12,000g and 4°C.
    - Carefully pipet off all supernatant.

    - Resuspend pellet in 1 mL 75% ethanol per 1 mL
      TRIzol reagent.
    - Vortex briefly.
    - Centrifuge for 5 min at 7,500g and 4°C.

    - Carefully pipet off all supernatant.
    - Air dry for 5-10 min.
    - Resuspend RNA in 20-50 µL water.
    - Incubate at 55-60°C for 10-15 min.

 7. Setup reverse transcription reactions (including
    −RT controls) for each sample [2]:

    - 1 µg RNA
    - 2 µL SuperScript VILO master mix (Invitrogen
      11755050)
    - water to 10 µL

 8. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 10 min
    - 50°C for 10 min
    - 85°C for 5 min
    - hold at 4°C

 9. Prepare −template and +template controls.

10. Setup 20 qPCR reactions [3,4]:

    Reagent                            Stock    Volume        22x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                     19.36 µL  425.92 µL

                                              44.00 µL   41.80 µL/rxn

    - Split into 2 identical 20 µL reactions.
    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

    Reagent                            Stock    Volume        22x

    water                                     19.36 µL  425.92 µL
    sz224,sz228 (5 extractions, ±RT)           2.20 µL

                                              44.00 µL   41.80 µL/rxn

    - Split into 2 identical 20 µL reactions.
    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

12. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 95°C for 30s
    - Repeat 40x:
      - 95°C for 10s
      - 61°C for 15s
      - Measure fluorescence
    - 65.0-95.0°C in 5s steps of 0.5°C:
      - Measure fluorescence
