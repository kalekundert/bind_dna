$ sw make p229 p230 p231

 1. Setup 1 restriction digestion [1,2]:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    water                         4.36 µL
    p224              275 ng/µL   3.64 µL
    rCutSmart Buffer        10x   1.00 µL
    BsrGI-HF            20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    EcoRI-HF            20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL

 2. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 80°C for 20 min

 3. Label the product: f182

 4. Purify using QIAquick PCR purification kit
    (28104) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a QIAquick column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 30.0 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

 5. Setup 3 ligation reactions [6]:

    Reagent              Stock   Volume     3.3x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                       1.17 µL  3.85 µL
    T4 ligase buffer       10x  0.50 µL  1.65 µL
    T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL  0.25 µL  0.83 µL
    T4 PNK             10 U/µL  0.25 µL  0.83 µL
    f182              20 ng/µL  0.83 µL  2.75 µL
    f165,f166,f167       90 nM  2.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL  3.00 µL/rxn

 6. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 15 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

 7. Label the products: p229, p230, p231

 8. Transform the following plasmids: p229, p230,
    p231 [7]

    - Pre-warm 3 LB+Amp plates.
    - For each transformation:

      - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
      - Add 1 µL plasmid.
      - Gently flick to mix.

      - Plate 25 µL cells.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

 9. Sequence the following plasmids:

    Plasmid  Primers
    ────────────────
    p229     sr5
    p230     sr5
    p231     sr5

10. Miniprep.
