1. Setup 4 restriction digestion reactions:

   - Use all combinations of the following reagents:

     NotI-HF: −, +
     HindIII-HF: −, +

   - Make 2x master mix:

     Reagent      Stock   Volume      4.4x
     ─────────────────────────────────────
     water               4.50 µL  19.80 µL
     p236     200 ng/µL  0.50 µL   2.20 µL
     ─────────────────────────────────────
                         5.00 µL  22.00 µL

   - Make XmnI mix:

     Reagent             Stock   Volume      48.4x
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────
     water                      3.85 µL  186.34 µL
     rCutSmart Buffer      10x  1.00 µL   48.40 µL
     XmnI              20 U/µL  0.05 µL    2.42 µL
     ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                4.90 µL  237.16 µL

   - Make 2 NotI-HF mixes:

     Reagent     Stock   Volume        22x
     ─────────────────────────────────────
     XmnI mix           4.90 µL  107.80 µL
     NotI-HF   20 U/µL  0.05 µL    1.10 µL
     ─────────────────────────────────────
                        4.95 µL  108.90 µL

   - Make 4 enzyme mixes:

     Reagent        Stock   Volume       10x
     ───────────────────────────────────────
     NotI-HF mix           4.95 µL  49.50 µL
     HindIII-HF   20 U/µL  0.05 µL   0.50 µL
     ───────────────────────────────────────
                           5.00 µL  50.00 µL

   - Setup the reactions:

     Reagent     Stock    Volume
     ───────────────────────────
     master mix     2x   5.00 µL
     enzyme mix          5.00 µL
     ───────────────────────────
                        10.00 µL

2. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

   - 37°C for 5–15 min
   - 80°C for 20 min

3. Prepare 4 samples for TBE PAGE:

   Reagent                             Stock    Volume
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
   TBE high-density sample buffer         5x   2.50 µL
   −/−,+/−,−/+,+/+                 200 ng/µL  10.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                              12.50 µL

4. Run a gel:

   gel: 4–20% TBE PAGE
   buffer: 1x TBE
   ladder: 1 µL 1 kb+ (NEB N3200, 1 µg/µL)
   samples: 10 µL/lane
   run: 200V for 60 min

5. Stain gel with GelGreen [1-3]:

   - Keep the stain protected from light.

   - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 30m.

6. Image with a laser scanner:

   laser:    488 nm
   filter:  518BP22

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[2] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[3] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
