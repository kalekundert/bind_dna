$ sw make p240

1. Setup 1 Gibson assembly [1]:

   Reagent               Stock   Volume
   ────────────────────────────────────
   Gibson master mix        2x  2.50 µL
   f192               89 ng/µL  0.50 µL
   f193               75 ng/µL  0.87 µL
   f194               40 ng/µL  1.13 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL

2. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

3. Label the product: p240

4. Transform p240 into MACH1 [2]:

   - Pre-warm 1 LB+Amp plate.
   - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
   - Add 1 µL plasmid.
   - Gently flick to mix.

   - Plate 25 µL cells.
   - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

5. Resuspend 8 colonies in 20 µL EB.  The suspension
   shouldn't be cloudy: you don't need a lot of
   template.

6. Setup 8 PCR reactions:

   - Use the following reagents:

     template DNA: colony #1, colony #2, colony #3,
       colony #4, colony #5, colony #6, colony #7,
       colony #8

   - Make 10x primer mix:

     Reagent   Stock   Volume       20x
     ──────────────────────────────────
     water            0.45 µL   9.00 µL
     o3       100 µM  0.03 µL   0.50 µL
     o358     100 µM  0.03 µL   0.50 µL
     ──────────────────────────────────
                      0.50 µL  10.00 µL

   - Make polymerase mix:

     Reagent        Stock   Volume     ≈9.1x
     ───────────────────────────────────────
     water                 1.50 µL  13.67 µL
     primer mix       10x  0.50 µL   4.56 µL
     Q5 master mix     2x  2.50 µL  22.78 µL
     ───────────────────────────────────────
                           4.50 µL  41.00 µL

   - Setup the reactions:

     Reagent         Stock   Volume
     ──────────────────────────────
     polymerase mix         4.50 µL
     template DNA           0.50 µL
     ──────────────────────────────
                            5.00 µL

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

7. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 55°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2m
   - Hold at 4°C

8. Run a 1% E-gel.  The expected amplicon size is 2.8
   kb.

9. Sequence p240 with .
