$ sw make p240

 1. Setup 1 reaction:

    Reagent        Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────
    water                 20.50 µL
    f193                   2.00 µL
    f194                   2.50 µL
    Q5 master mix     2x  25.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────
                          50.00 µL

 2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 14x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 72°C for 20s; -0.5°C each cycle
      - 72°C for 1m30
    - 72°C for 2m

 3. Setup 1 PCR reaction:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    water                        20.00 µL
    previous reaction  20 pg/µL   4.00 µL
    o396                 100 µM   0.50 µL
    o397                 100 µM   0.50 µL
    Q5 master mix            2x  25.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                 50.00 µL

 4. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 17x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 72°C for 20s; -0.5°C each cycle
      - 72°C for 1m30
    - Repeat 23x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 64°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 1m30
    - 72°C for 2m

 5. Label the product: f199

 6. Purify using QIAquick PCR purification kit
    (28104) [1,2]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a QIAquick column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 50 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

 7. Setup 1 Gibson assembly [3]:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    Gibson master mix        2x  2.50 µL
    f192               89 ng/µL  0.47 µL
    f199               50 ng/µL  2.03 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                 5.00 µL

 8. Incubate at 50°C for 15 min.

 9. Label the product: p240

10. Transform p240 into MACH1 [4]:

    - Pre-warm 1 LB+Amp plate.
    - Thaw 25 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
    - Add 1 µL plasmid.
    - Gently flick to mix.

    - Plate 25 µL cells.
    - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

11. Resuspend 8 colonies in 20 µL EB.  The suspension
    shouldn't be cloudy: you don't need a lot of
    template.

12. Setup 8 PCR reactions:

    - Use the following reagents:

      template DNA: colony #1, colony #2, colony #3,
        colony #4, colony #5, colony #6, colony #7,
        colony #8

    - Make 10x primer mix:

      Reagent   Stock   Volume       20x
      ──────────────────────────────────
      water            0.45 µL   9.00 µL
      o3       100 µM  0.03 µL   0.50 µL
      o358     100 µM  0.03 µL   0.50 µL
      ──────────────────────────────────
                       0.50 µL  10.00 µL

    - Make polymerase mix:

      Reagent        Stock   Volume     ≈9.1x
      ───────────────────────────────────────
      water                 1.50 µL  13.67 µL
      primer mix       10x  0.50 µL   4.56 µL
      Q5 master mix     2x  2.50 µL  22.78 µL
      ───────────────────────────────────────
                            4.50 µL  41.00 µL

    - Setup the reactions:

      Reagent         Stock   Volume
      ──────────────────────────────
      polymerase mix         4.50 µL
      template DNA           0.50 µL
      ──────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

13. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 55°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 1m30
    - 72°C for 2m
    - Hold at 4°C

14. Run a 1% E-gel.  The expected amplicon size is
    2.8 kb.

15. Sequence p240 with .
