     Reagent   Stock   Volume       20x

   - Setup 1 reaction:

     Reagent           Stock   Volume       11x
     ──────────────────────────────────────────
     water                    1.50 µL  16.50 µL
     p242           20 pg/µL  0.50 µL   5.50 µL
     primer mix          10x  0.50 µL   5.50 µL
     Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL  27.50 µL
     ──────────────────────────────────────────
                              5.00 µL  55.00 µL

   - Split into 10 identical 5.00 µL reactions.

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 56–71°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m
   - 72°C for 2m
   - Hold at 4°C

3. Run a 1% E-gel.
