    Reagent               Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────

 3. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 80°C for 20 min

 4. Perform a serial dilution [4]:

    - Put 70.72 µL 55 ng/µL f202 in a tube.
    - Put 43.75 µL water in 2 adjacent tubes.
    - Transfer 26.97 µL between each tube to make 3
      2.6-fold dilutions.

 5. Concentrate the remaining f202 digestion to 60 µL
    by lyophilization.

 6. Perform a serial dilution [5]:

    - Put 61.00 µL 100 ng/µL f202 (lyo) in a tube.
    - Put 43.75 µL water in 2 adjacent tubes.
    - Transfer 17.25 µL between each tube to make 3
      3.5-fold dilutions.

 7. Perform a serial dilution [6]:

    - Put 54.69 µL 200 ng/µL f203 in a tube.
    - Put 43.75 µL water in 2 adjacent tubes.
    - Transfer 10.94 µL between each tube to make 3
      5-fold dilutions.

 8. Prepare 9 samples for electrophoresis:

    Reagent                 Stock    Volume      13.5x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    f202, f202 (lyo), f203         43.75 µL  590.62 µL
    loading dye                6x   8.75 µL  118.12 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   52.50 µL   52.50 µL/rxn

 9. Run a gel:

    gel: 0.5% agarose
    buffer: 0.5x tris-acetate
    ladder: 10 µL 50 ng/µL 1kb+ DNA ladder
    samples: 60 µL/lane
    run: 100V for 40 min

10. Stain gel with GelGreen [7-9]:

    - Keep the stain protected from light.

    - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM
      NaCl.
    - Shake gently for 30m.

11. Purify the desired band using the QIAEX II kit:

    - Cut the 2.8 kb (f202) or 4.1 kb (f203) band out
      of the gel.

    - Add 3 volumes QX1.
    - Add 30 µL QIAEX II beads.
      - Resuspend the beads by vortexing for 30s.
    - Incubate at 50°C for 10 min.
      - Vortex every 2 min to keep the beads
        suspended.
      - If the solution is not yellow after the
        agarose dissolves, add 10 µL 3M sodium
        acetate pH=5 and continue incubating for at
        least 5 min.
    - Centrifuge for 30s.  Discard supernatant.

    - Add 500 µL QX1.
    - Vortex to resuspend the pellet.
    - Centrifuge for 30s.  Discard supernatant.

    - Repeat 2x:
      - Add 500 µL PE.
      - Vortex to resuspend the pellet.
      - Centrifuge for 30s.  Discard supernatant.

    - Air-dry the pellet for 30 min.

    - Add 20 µL EB.
    - Incubate at room temperature for 5 min.
    - Centrifuge for 30s.  Transfer supernatant to a
      clean tube.
