September 21, 2022

$ sw make f191

 1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    sr4      100 µM   0.50 µL
    sr5      100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

 2. Setup 1 PCR reaction [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────
    water                     9.50 µL
    l2             20 ng/µL   0.50 µL
    primer mix          10x   2.50 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  12.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────
                             25.00 µL

 3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 6x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 61°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 15s
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 4. Label the product: l4

 5. Check if the PCR reaction produced enough product:
    
    - Run 1 µL of the PCR reaction on a 1% E-gel.
    - If no band is visible, run two additional PCR 
      cycles and repeat this check.

 6. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 7. Setup 1 BbsI-HF digestion [6,7]:

    Reagent             Stock      Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────
    l4                1 ng/µL  1000.00 µL
    rCutSmart Buffer      10x   111.17 µL
    BbsI-HF           20 U/µL     0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────
                                  1.11 mL

 8. Incubate at the following temperatures [8]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 65°C for 20 min

 9. Label the product: f191

10. Prepare 1 sample for TBE PAGE:

    Reagent                           Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    TBE high-density sample buffer       5x   2.00 µL
    f191                            1 ng/µL   8.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                             10.00 µL

11. Run a gel:

    gel: 4–20% TBE PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL 1 kb+ (NEB N3200)
    samples: 10 µL/lane
    run: 200V for 60 min

12. Cut the desired band out of the gel [9].

    - Place the gel over a TLC plate.
    - Use a UV light to visualize the RNA (dark
      spot).
    - Consider visualizing remaining gel to ensure
      that all desired RNA was excised.

13. Crush the gel slice.

    - Poke a hole in the bottom of a 0.65 mL tube
      with a 27 g needle.
    - Place gel slice inside the 0.65 mL tube.
    - Place the 0.65 mL tube inside a 1.5 mL tube.
    - Spin at max speed for 5 min.

14. Resuspend gel in 400 µL PAGE elution buffer:

    Reagent               Stock     Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          439.00 µL
    Tris, pH 7.5            1 M    5.00 µL
    NaCl                    5 M   50.00 µL
    EDTA                 500 mM    1.00 µL
    SDS                     10%    5.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────
                                 500.00 µL

15. Incubate at 4°C overnight with end-over-end
    mixing.

16. Remove gel debris by spin filtration:

    - Load samples onto a 0.22 µm cellose-acetate
      Spin-X column [10].
    - Spin at 13,000g for 5 min.

17. Purify using Zymo Clean & Concentrator-5 kit
    (R1015) [11,12]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 16000g.
    - Add 800 µL RNA binding buffer to the crude RNA.
    - Add 1200 µL 95-100% ethanol to the crude RNA.
    - Load on a Zymo-Spin IICR column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 700 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 15 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

Notes:
 [1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
     100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

 [2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

 [3] For diluting template DNA to 20 ng/µL:
     Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

 [4] https://www.qiagen.com/HB-2069

 [5] Column capacity: 5 µg

 [6] https://www.neb.com/products/r3539-bbsi-hf

 [7] NEB recommends 5–10 units of enzyme per µg DNA
     (10–20 units for genomic DNA).  Enzyme volume
     should not exceed 10% of the total reaction
     volume to prevent star activity due to excess
     glycerol.

 [8] The heat inactivation step is not necessary if
     the DNA will be purified before use.

 [9] Based on Fitzy's DNA PAGE purification protocol,
     [Nilson2013], and [Petrov2013].

[10] Nylon and cellulose nitrate have high DNA-
     binding: https://tinyurl.com/3pkyc8dr

[11] https://tinyurl.com/yd699u8b

[12] Column capacity: 10 µg
