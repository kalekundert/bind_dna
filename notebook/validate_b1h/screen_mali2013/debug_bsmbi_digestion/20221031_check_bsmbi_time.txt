1. Setup 3 restriction digestion reactions:

   - Make 2x BsmBI mix:

     Reagent          Stock   Volume     ≈33.3x
     ──────────────────────────────────────────
     water                   3.98 µL  132.83 µL
     NEBuffer r3.1      10x  1.00 µL   33.33 µL
     BsmBI-v2       10 U/µL  0.01 µL    0.50 µL
     ──────────────────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL  166.67 µL

   - Setup 1 reaction:

     Reagent       Stock    Volume      3.3x
     ───────────────────────────────────────
     water                 4.00 µL  13.20 µL
     f206       15 ng/µL   1.00 µL   3.30 µL
     BsmBI mix        2x   5.00 µL  16.50 µL
     ───────────────────────────────────────
                          10.00 µL  33.00 µL

   - Split into 3 identical 10.00 µL reactions.

2. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

   - 55°C for 15, 60, and 120 min
   - 80°C for 20 min

3. Prepare 3 samples for TBE PAGE:

   Reagent                             Stock    Volume
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
   TBE high-density sample buffer         5x   2.50 µL
   15m,1h,2h                       200 ng/µL  10.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                              12.50 µL

4. Run a gel:

   gel: 4–20% TBE PAGE
   buffer: 1x TBE
   ladder: 5 µL TriDye ultra low range ladder
   samples: 10 µL/lane
   run: 200V for 60 min

5. Stain gel with GelGreen [1-3]:

   - Keep the stain protected from light.

   - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 1x GelGreen, 1x running
     buffer.
   - Shake gently for 30m.

6. Image with a laser scanner:

   laser:    520 nm
   filter:  565BP24

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[2] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[3] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
