$ sw make_f206

 1. Setup 9 PCR reactions [1-3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume      9.9x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
    water                     6.24 µL  61.78 µL
    o400           20 pg/µL   1.60 µL  15.84 µL
    sr2              100 µM   0.08 µL   0.79 µL
    sr3              100 µM   0.08 µL   0.79 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x   8.00 µL  79.20 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
                             16.00 µL  16.00 µL/rxn

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

 2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 54-69°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 10s
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 3. Prepare 1 sample for TBE PAGE:

    Reagent                            Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    TBE high-density sample buffer        5x   4.00 µL
    f206                            80 ng/µL  16.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                              20.00 µL

 4. Run a gel:

    gel: 4–20% TBE PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL 1 kb+ (NEB N3200, 1 µg/µL)
    samples: 20 µL/lane
    run: 200V for 60 min

 5. Cut the desired band out of the gel [4].

    - Place the gel over a TLC plate.
    - Use a UV light to visualize the RNA (dark
      spot).
    - Consider visualizing remaining gel to ensure
      that all desired RNA was excised.

 6. Crush the gel slice.

    - Poke a hole in the bottom of a 0.65 mL tube
      with a 27 g needle.
    - Place gel slice inside the 0.65 mL tube.
    - Place the 0.65 mL tube inside a 1.5 mL tube.
    - Spin at max speed for 5 min.

 7. Resuspend gel in 400 µL PAGE elution buffer:

    Reagent               Stock     Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          439.00 µL
    Tris, pH 7.5            1 M    5.00 µL
    NaCl                    5 M   50.00 µL
    EDTA                 500 mM    1.00 µL
    SDS                     10%    5.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────
                                 500.00 µL

 8. Incubate at 55°C overnight with 800 rpm mixing.

 9. Remove gel debris by spin filtration:

    - Load samples onto a 0.22 µm cellose-acetate
      Spin-X column [5].
    - Spin at 13,000g for 5 min.

10. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
    (28204) [6,7]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
    - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
      pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.
