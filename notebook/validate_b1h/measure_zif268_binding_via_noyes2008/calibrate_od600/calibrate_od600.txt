1. Grow an overnight culture of a freshly- 
   transformed s5 colony in LB+Carb+Kan.

2. Inoculate 4 day cultures by diluting the 
   following volumes of overnight culture into
   3 mL NM+His+Ura+Carb+Kan:

   - 10 µL
   - 30 µL
   - 90 µL
   - 270 µL

3. Incubate day cultures at 37°C with shaking at 
   225 rpm.

4. Measure the OD600 of each culture every hour.
