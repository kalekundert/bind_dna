$ sw make p190 p191 p192

 1. Setup 1 restriction digestion [1,2]:

    Reagent              Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────
    p168              75 ng/µL  13.33 µL
    rCutSmart Buffer       10x   1.59 µL
    EcoRI-HF           20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    NotI-HF            20 U/µL   0.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────
                                15.93 µL

 2. Incubate at the following temperatures [3]:

    - 37°C for 5–15 min
    - 65°C for 20 min

 3. Label the product: f142

 4. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 5. Setup 3 annealing reactions:

    Reagent          Stock   Volume
    ───────────────────────────────
    PBS                10x  0.40 µL
    o298,o300,o302  100 µM  1.80 µL
    0299,o301,o303  100 µM  1.80 µL
    ───────────────────────────────
                            4.00 µL

 6. Perform the annealing reactions:

    - Incubate at 95°C for 2 min.
    - Cool at room temperature.

 7. Label the products: f155, f156, f157

 8. Dilute f155, f156, and f157 to 90 nM.

 9. Setup 3 ligation reactions [6]:

    Reagent              Stock   Volume     3.3x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
    T4 ligase buffer       10x  0.50 µL  1.65 µL
    T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL  0.25 µL  0.83 µL
    T4 PNK             10 U/µL  0.25 µL  0.83 µL
    f142              80 ng/µL  2.29 µL  7.57 µL
    f155,f156,f157       90 nM  1.71 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
                                5.00 µL  3.29 µL/rxn

10. Incubate at the following temperatures:

    - 25°C for 15 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

11. Label the products: p190, p191, p192

12. Transform the following plasmids: p190, p191,
    p192 [7]

    - Pre-warm 3 LB+Kan plates.
    - For each transformation:

      - Thaw 10 µL competent MACH1 cells on ice.
      - Add 2 µL plasmid.
      - Gently flick to mix
      - Incubate on ice for 30 min.

      - Incubate at 42°C for 30s.
      - Incubate on ice for 2 min.
      - Add 90 µL SOC.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 60 min with end-over-end
        mixing.

      - Plate 100 µL cells.
      - Incubate at 37°C for 16h.

13. Sequence the following plasmids:

    Plasmid  Primers
    ────────────────
    p190     o251
    p191     o251
    p192     o251

14. Miniprep.
