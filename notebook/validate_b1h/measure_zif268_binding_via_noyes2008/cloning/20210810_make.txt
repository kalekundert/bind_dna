$ sw make p167 p168 p169 -x p180

1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent   Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────
   water             9.00 µL
   o248     100 µM   0.50 µL
   o249     100 µM   0.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────
                    10.00 µL

2. Setup 3 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent            Stock   Volume        5x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                     1.50 µL   7.50 µL
   p179,p180,p181  20 pg/µL  0.50 µL
   primer mix           10x  0.50 µL   2.50 µL
   Q5 master mix         2x  2.50 µL  12.50 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                             5.00 µL   4.50 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 3 min
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Label the products: f131, f132, f133

5. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
   (28204) [4,5]:

   - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
   - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
   - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
   - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
     pH=5.0.
   - Load on a MinElute column.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

   - Add 750 µL PE.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Add 10 µL EB.
   - Wait at least 1m.
   - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

6. Setup 3 Golden Gate assemblies [6]:

   Reagent              Stock   Volume     3.3x
   ────────────────────────────────────────────
   T4 ligase buffer       10x  0.50 µL  1.65 µL
   T4 DNA ligase     400 U/µL  0.12 µL  0.41 µL
   BsaI-HFv2          20 U/µL  0.12 µL  0.41 µL
   f130              10 ng/µL  0.21 µL  0.68 µL
   f131,f132,f133    25 ng/µL  4.04 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────
                               5.00 µL  0.96 µL/rxn

7. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 37°C for 5 min

   Or, to maximize the number of transformants:

   - 37°C for 60 min
   - 60°C for 5 min

8. Label the products: p167, p168, p169

9. Miniprep.

Notes:

    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[5] Column capacity: 5 µg
