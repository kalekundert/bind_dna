$ sw pick_seq_primers

1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent                    Stock    Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
   water                              9.00 µL
   o246                      100 µM   0.50 µL
   o247,o250,o251,o252,o253  100 µM   0.50 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
                                     10.00 µL

2. Setup 5 PCR reactions [2,3]:

   Reagent           Stock   Volume        7x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
   water                    1.50 µL  10.50 µL
   p181           20 pg/µL  0.50 µL   3.50 µL
   primer mix          10x  0.50 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  2.50 µL  17.50 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────
                            5.00 µL   4.50 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

4. Run a 1% E-gel.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL
