$ stepwise test_staining

1. Prepare 500 ng/µL BSA:
   - 199 µL water
   - 1 µL 0.1% BSA

2. Prepare known DNA and protein samples:

   Reagent                 Stock   Volume       18x
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                          1.50 µL  27.00 µL
   Bolt LDS sample buffer     4x  2.50 µL  45.00 µL
   Bolt reducing agent       10x  1.00 µL  18.00 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                  5.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

   Reagent                        Stock    Volume      6.6x
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                  4.00 µL  26.40 µL
   loading buffer master mix         2x   5.00 µL  33.00 µL
   BSA                        500 ng/µL   1.00 µL   6.60 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         10.00 µL  10.00 µL/rxn

   Reagent                   Stock    Volume      6.6x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
   loading buffer master        2x   5.00 µL  33.00 µL
   1 kb Plus DNA ladder   50 ng/µL   5.00 µL  33.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    10.00 µL  10.00 µL/rxn

3. Load the gel such that it can be cut into 6
   pieces, each with a protein lane and a DNA lane.

4. Run a gel:

   - Use a 4−12% SDS PAGE gel.
   - Load 10 µL of each sample.
   - Run at 165V for 42 min.

5. Cut the gel as described above.  Identify each 
   piece by notching the corners according to a 
   binary code:

   - Top left:     8 if notched
   - Top right:    4 if notched
   - Bottom left:  2 if notched
   - Bottom right: 1 if notched

6. Prepare 90 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.

7. Stain the gel pieces with the following protocols:

   0: Coomassie only
   1: GelGreen only (− wash)
   2: GelGreen only (+ wash)
   3: Coomassie then GelGreen
   4: GelGreen then Coomassie (− wash)
   5: GelGreen then Coomassie (+ wash)

   0,2,3,5: Wash the gel

   - Repeat 3 times:
     - Add 100 mL water.
     - Microwave until almost boiling (1 min).
     - Shake gently for 1 min.

   1,4: Stain with GelGreen (− wash) [1]

   - Rinse gel with water.
   - Submerge gel in 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 30 min.

   2,5: Stain with GelGreen (+ wash) [1]

   - Submerge gel in 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 30 min.

   0,3: Stain with Coomassie:

   - Submerge gel in SimplyBlue SafeStain.
   - Microwave until almost boiling (45-60s).
   - Shake gently for 5 min.
   - Discard stain and add 100 mL water.
   - Shake gently for 10 min.

   0: Wash with salt:

   - Add 20 mL 5M NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 5 min.

   3: Stain with GelGreen:

   - Submerge gel in 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 30 min.

   4,5: Stain with Coomassie

   - Submerge gel in SimplyBlue SafeStain.
   - Microwave until almost boiling (45-60s).
   - Shake gently for 5 min.
   - Discard stain and add 100 mL water.
   - Shake gently for 10 min.
   - Add 20 mL 5M NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 5 min.

8. Image with a laser scanner:

   lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
   filters:  518BP22  710BP40

Note:
[1] Stain the washed and unwashed gel pieces
    separately, because it's possible that the excess
    SDS from the unwashed gels could interfere with
    staining.
