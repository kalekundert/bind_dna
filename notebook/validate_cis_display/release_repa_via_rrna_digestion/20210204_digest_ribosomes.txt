$ stepwise digest_ribosomes

1. Setup 4 PURExpress reactions:

   Reagent          Stock   Volume     4.4x
   ────────────────────────────────────────
   water                   1.10 µL  4.84 µL
   A                       2.00 µL  8.80 µL
   B                       1.50 µL  6.60 µL
   f62,f63,f65,f66  75 nM  0.40 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────
                           5.00 µL  4.60 µL/rxn

   - Keep on ice.
   - Be sure to add A before B.
   - The control template (125 ng/µL) is 75 nM.

2. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

3. Add 0.5 µL RNase A/RNase T1 cocktail to each
   reaction.

4. Divide each reaction into 2x 2.5 µL aliquots.

5. Incubate the aliquots at 37°C and 65°C
   respectively for 15 min.

6. Prepare 8 samples for native PAGE:

   Reagent        Stock   Volume       12x
   ───────────────────────────────────────
   water                 3.25 µL  39.00 µL
   sample buffer     4x  1.50 µL  18.00 µL
   sample                1.25 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────
                         6.00 µL   4.75 µL/rxn

7. Run a gel:

   - Gel: 3−12% Bis-Tris Novex NativePAGE
   - Buffer: NativePAGE running buffer
   - Load 5 µL of each sample.
   - Run at 150V for 115 min.

8. Image with a laser scanner:

   lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
   filters:  518BP22  710BP40
