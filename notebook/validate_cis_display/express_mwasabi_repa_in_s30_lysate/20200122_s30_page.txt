$ stepwise s30 f3,f8
$ stepwise page native 2

1. Thaw 1 S30 extract aliquot [1].

2. Setup the S30 extract reaction:

   Reagent  Stock    Volume     2.2x
   ─────────────────────────────────
   water            1.20 µL  2.64 µL
   A          10x   1.00 µL  2.20 µL
   B         2.5x   4.00 µL  8.80 µL
   C        3.33x   3.00 µL  6.60 µL
   f3,f8    75 nM   0.80 µL         
   ─────────────────────────────────
                   10.00 µL  9.20 µL/rxn

   - Vortex gently and spin to collect.

3. Incubate at 37°C for 1h [2].

4. Stop reaction by incubating at 4°C.

5. Prepare samples for native PAGE:

   Reagent        Stock   Volume       3x
   ──────────────────────────────────────
   water                 2.50 µL  7.50 µL
   sample buffer     4x  2.50 µL  7.50 µL
   DNA/protein           1.25 µL         
   ──────────────────────────────────────
                         6.25 µL  5.00 µL/rxn

   - For a DNA ladder, use 5 µL 50 ng/µL.

6. Run the gel:

   - Use a 3−12% native PAGE gel.
   - Load 5 µL of each sample.
   - Run at 150V for 115 min.

Notes:
[1] A: Complete amino acids
    B: S30 premix without amino acids
    C: S30 extract, linear

[2] Enhanced expression at lower temperatures for 
    longer times appears to be gene/protein-specific 
    and may be tested if the standard reaction at 37°C 
    for 1 hour does not produce the desired results.
