1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

   Reagent   Stock    Volume
   ─────────────────────────
   water            12.00 µL
   o186     100 µM   4.00 µL
   o189     100 µM   4.00 µL
   ─────────────────────────
                    20.00 µL

2. Perform a serial dilution [2]:

   - Put 20.00 μL 10 x primer mix in a tube.
   - Put 10.00 μL water in 2 adjacent tubes.
   - Transfer 10.00 μL between each tube to make 3
     2-fold dilutions.

3. Setup 3 PCR reactions [3,4]:

   Reagent           Stock    Volume     ≈3.4x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
   water                     7.20 µL  24.60 µL
   p124           20 pg/µL   2.40 µL   8.20 µL
   primer mix          10x   2.40 µL
   Q5 master mix        2x  12.00 µL  41.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────
                            24.00 µL  21.60 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

4. Split each reaction into two 10 µL reactions, one
   for each of the following thermocyler protocols.

5. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

6. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 45x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 60°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

7. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
   (28204) [5,6]:

   - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
   - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
   - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
   - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
     pH=5.0.
   - Load on a MinElute column.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

   - Add 750 µL PE.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
   - Add 10 µL EB.
   - Wait at least 1m.
   - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

8. Measure yield by NanoDrop.

Notes:

    100 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] The final concentrations will be:
    1  1.000000e+01 x
    2  5.000000e+00 x
    3  2.500000e+00 x

[4] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[6] Column capacity: 5 µg
