$ stepwise purexpress f61 f62 f63 f64 f65 f66 -v 5
$ stepwise gels/gel agarose15 6 -a 'wells in the middle'
$ stepwise gels/laser_scanner blue red

1. Setup 6 PURExpress reactions:

   Reagent                    Stock   Volume      6.6x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                             1.00 µL   6.60 µL
   A                                 2.00 µL  13.20 µL
   B                                 1.50 µL   9.90 µL
   RNase Inhibitor          40 U/µL  0.10 µL   0.66 µL
   f61,f62,f63,f64,f65,f66    75 nM  0.40 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                     5.00 µL   4.60 µL/rxn

   - Keep on ice.
   - Be sure to add A before B.
   - The control template (125 ng/µL) is 75 nM.

2. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

3. Prepare samples for electrophoresis [1]:

   Reagent         Stock   Volume       9x
   ───────────────────────────────────────
   loading buffer     6x  1.00 µL  9.00 µL
   sample                 5.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────
                          6.00 µL  1.00 µL/rxn

4. Run a gel:

   - Use a 1.5% agarose gel with wells in the middle.
   - Load 5 µL of each sample.
   - Run at 100V for 60 min.

5. Image with a laser scanner:

   lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
   filters:  518BP22  710BP40

Note:
[1] I haven't used these electrophoresis parameters
    before.  It might take some trial and error to
    get them right.
