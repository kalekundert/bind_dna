   Reagent                                                    Stock    Volume      12.1x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                                                             19.50 µL  235.95 µL
   p108,p109,p110,p111,p112,p113,p114,p115,p116,p117,p118  20 pg/µL   5.00 µL

   Q5 master mix                                                 2x  25.00 µL  302.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                                                     50.00 µL   45.00 µL/rxn

   - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

   - 98°C for 30s
   - Repeat 35x:
     - 98°C for 10s
     - 65°C for 20s
     - 72°C for 1m30
   - 72°C for 2 min
   - 4°C hold

3. Label the products:
   f50,f51,f52,f53,f54,f55,f56,f57,f58,f59,f60

4. Purify amplicons by PCR cleanup.

   - Elute in 10 µL EB.
