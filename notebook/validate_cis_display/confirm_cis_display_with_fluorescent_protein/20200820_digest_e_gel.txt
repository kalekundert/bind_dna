$ stepwise digest p153 EcoRI-HF -v 10 -d 0.5 -D 750 -n 4
$ stepwise e_gel

1. Setup 4 EcoRI-HF digestions [1]:

   Reagent              Stock    Volume      4.4x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                        8.08 µL  35.57 µL
   p153             750 ng/µL   0.67 µL
   CutSmart Buffer        10x   1.00 µL   4.40 µL
   EcoRI-HF           20 U/µL   0.25 µL   1.10 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                               10.00 µL   9.33 µL/rxn

2. Incubate at the following temperatures [2]:

   - 37°C for 5–15 min
   - 65°C for 20 min

3. Run a 1% E-gel.

Notes:
[1] NEB recommends 5–10 units of enzyme per µg DNA 
    (10–20 units for genomic DNA).  Enzyme volume 
    should not exceed 10% of the total reaction 
    volume to prevent star activity due to excess 
    glycerol.

[2] The heat inactivation step is not necessary if 
    the DNA will be purified before use.
