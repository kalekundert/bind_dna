 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    PURExpress:          +  +  +
    mRNA+linker (f146):  +  −  −
    mRNA (f145):         −  +  −

 3. Setup 3 PURExpress (NEB E6800) reactions [1]:

    Reagent                    Stock    Volume      3.3x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    solution A                         4.00 µL  13.20 µL
    solution B                         3.00 µL   9.90 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine  40 U/µL   0.20 µL   0.66 µL
    FluoroTect GreenLys                0.50 µL   1.65 µL
    f146,f145,Ø              2500 nM   2.30 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                      10.00 µL   7.70 µL/rxn

    - Thaw all components on ice.
    - Mix A well; certain components may precipitate
      during storage.
    - Mix B gently; do not vortex.
    - Add A before B.

 4. Incubate at 37°C for 90 min.

 5. Incubate on ice for 10 min.

 6. Add 3.15 µL of the following salt solution to
    each reaction:

    - 3 µL 2M MgCl₂
    - 20 µL 3M KCl

 7. Incubate as follows:

    - RT for 10 min
    - −20°C overnight

 8. Desalt using Zeba 75 µL spin columns [2]:
    
    - Remove column's bottom enclosure and loosen 
      (but don't remove) cap.
    - Place column in clean microcentrifuge tube.
    - Spin 1000g, 1 min.
    - Discard flow-through.
    
    - Repeat 3x:
      - Add 50 µL water.
      - Spin 1000g, 1 min [3].
      - Discard flow-through.
    
    - Blot bottom of column to remove excess 
      liquid.
    - Place column in new collection tube.
    - Apply 2-12 µL sample to the resin bed [4].
    - Apply 3 µL water or buffer to the resin bed 
      after the sample has absorbed.
    - Spin 1000g, 2 min to collect desalted sample.

 9. Prepare 3 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                 Stock    Volume      4.5x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                           1.50 µL   6.75 µL
    Bolt LDS sample buffer     4x   2.50 µL  11.25 µL
    Bolt reducing agent       10x   1.00 µL   4.50 µL
    sample                          5.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   10.00 µL   5.00 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

10. Run a gel:

    gel: 4–12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    ladder: 5 µL PageRuler unstained protein ladder
    samples: 10 µL/lane
    run: 165V for 42 min

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    laser:    488 nm
    filter:  518BP22

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/dxapyo69

[2] https://tinyurl.com/4ud2p29e

[3] After each spin, the resin should appear white 
    and free of liquid. If liquid is present, make 
    sure you are using the correct centrifugation 
    speed and time. Incomplete centrifugation may 
    result in poor sample recovery or sample 
    dilution.

[4] In general, reducing the sample volume added 
    to the column increases small molecule removal,
    and increasing sample volume maximizes protein 
    recovery.
