1. Make 2.42x 10 µL reaction aliquots of PURExpress 
   (NEB E6800) [1]:
   
   Solution A: 9.68 µL
   Solution B: 7.26 µL

2. Setup 2 PURExpress (NEB E6800) reactions [1]:

   Reagent                    Stock    Volume     2.5x
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                              1.50 µL  3.75 µL
   solution A                         4.00 µL
   solution B                         3.00 µL
   RNase inhibitor, murine  40 U/µL   0.20 µL  0.50 µL
   FluoroTect GreenLys                0.50 µL  1.25 µL
   DHFR                       75 nM   0.80 µL  2.00 µL
   ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                     10.00 µL  3.00 µL/rxn

   - Setup the above reaction with both new and old
     PURExpress.
   - Thaw all components on ice.
   - Mix A well; certain components may precipitate
     during storage.
   - Mix B gently; do not vortex.
   - Add A before B.

3. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

4. Add 1 µL RNase cocktail (Invitrogen AM2286) to
   each 10 µL IVTT reaction.

5. Incubate at 37°C for 15 min.

6. Prepare 2 samples for SDS PAGE:

   Reagent                 Stock    Volume        3x
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                           4.00 µL  12.00 µL
   Bolt LDS sample buffer     4x   2.50 µL   7.50 µL
   Bolt reducing agent       10x   1.00 µL   3.00 µL
   sample                          2.50 µL
   ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                  10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

   - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

7. Run a gel:

   gel: 4–12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
   buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
   ladder: 5 µL SeeBlue Plus2 (Invitrogen LC5925)
   samples: 10 µL/lane
   run: 165V for 42 min

8. Image with a laser scanner:

   laser:    488 nm
   filter:  518BP22
