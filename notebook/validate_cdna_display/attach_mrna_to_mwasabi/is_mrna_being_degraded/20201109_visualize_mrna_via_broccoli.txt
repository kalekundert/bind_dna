1. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

   - Put 2.60 μL 2842.95 ng/µL f97 in the first tube.
   - Add 2.00 μL nuclease-free water in the 7 remaining tubes.
   - Transfer 0.60 μL between each tube to make
     8 4.3-fold dilutions.

2. Prepare samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

   Reagent            Stock    Volume
   ──────────────────────────────────
   water                      4.00 µL
   sample buffer         2x   5.00 µL
   f97            200 ng/µL   1.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────
                             10.00 µL

   - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

3. Run a gel:

   - Use a 6% TBE/urea PAGE gel.
   - Load 10 µL of each sample.
   - Run at 180V for 40 min.

4. Stain gel with DFHBI-1T [2]:

   - Repeat 3 times:
     - Add 100 mL water
     - Shake gently for 5 min

   - Submerge gel in DFHBI-1T buffer [3].
   - Shake gently for 10-30 min.

5. Image with a laser scanner:

   laser:   488 nm
   filter:  518BP22
