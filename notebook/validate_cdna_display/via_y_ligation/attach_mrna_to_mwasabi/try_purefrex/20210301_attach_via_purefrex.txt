 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    PUREfrex:    −   +    +      +    +
    f89:         +   +    +      +    +
    MgOAc [mM]:  0  750  375   187.5  0
    KCl [mM]:    0  65   32.5  16.25  0

 3. Prepare a 3.17x salt solution:

    - 5.00 µL 1M MgOAc
    - 19.23 µL 3M KCl

 4. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 20.00 μL 3.17 x salt solution in a tube.
    - Put 10.00 μL nuclease-free water in 3 adjacent
      tubes.
    - Transfer 10.00 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 3 2-fold dilutions.

 5. Setup 1 PUREfrex 2.0 (GeneFrontier PF201)
    reaction [2]:

    Reagent                    Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
    solution I                        11.00 µL
    solution II                        1.10 µL
    solution III                       2.20 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine  40 U/µL   0.20 µL
    f89                      1000 nM   7.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
                                      22.00 µL

    - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
      on ice.
    - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
    - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

 6. Incubate at 37°C for 30 min.

 7. Setup 4 coupling reactions:

    - 5.0 µL translation reaction
    - 2.3 µL salt dilution

 8. Incubate at 25°C for 1h.

 9. Prepare 5 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                  Stock    Volume      7.5x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                            4.00 µL  30.00 µL
    Bolt LDS sample buffer      4x   2.50 µL  18.75 µL
    Bolt reducing agent        10x   1.00 µL   7.50 µL
    sample                  160 nM   2.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

10. Run a gel:

    - Gel: 4−12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    - Buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    - Load 10 µL of each sample.
    - Run at 165V for 42 min.

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40

12. Incubate the remaining coupling reaction overnight
    at −20°C, and run the same gel as above the next
    day.
