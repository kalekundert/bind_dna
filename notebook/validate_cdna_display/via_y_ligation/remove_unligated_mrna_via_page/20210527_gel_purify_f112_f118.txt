$ sw gel_purify_f112_f118

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. Setup 2 ligation reactions:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume      2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water           6.00 µL  13.20 µL
    T4 RNA ligase buffer    10x   5.00 µL  11.00 µL
    ATP                   10 mM   5.00 µL  11.00 µL
    f111,f11              10 µM  20.00 µL
    o236,o128             10 µM  12.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 48.00 µL  16.00 µL/rxn

 3. Incubate as follows:

    - 90°C for 30s
    - Cool to 25°C at 1°C/s

 4. Add to each reaction:

    - 2 µL 10 U/µL T4 RNA ligase

 5. Incubate at 25°C for 30 min.

 6. Label the products: f112, f118

 7. Concentrate the reaction to ≈5 µL by
    lyophilization.

 8. Prepare 2 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent          Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────
    RNA loading dye     2x   5.00 µL
    f112,f118        10 µM   5.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────
                            10.00 µL

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

 9. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL low range ssRNA ladder (NEB N0364S)
    run: 180V for 40 min

10. Cut the ligated and unligated mRNA bands out of
    the gel [1].

    - Place the gel over a TLC plate.
    - Use a UV light to visualize the RNA (dark spot)
    - Consider visualizing remaining gel to ensure
      that all desired RNA was excised.

11. Crush gel slices.

    - Poke 3-4 holes in 0.65 mL tube with 27 g
      needle.
    - Place gel slice inside 0.65 mL tube.
    - Place 0.65 mL tube inside 1.5 mL tube.
    - Centrifuge 5 min at max speed.

12. Resuspend gel in 400 µL PAGE elution buffer

    - Tris-HCl, pH 7.5 10 mM
    - NaCl 500 mM
    - EDTA 1 mM
    - SDS 0.1%

13. Incubate at 4°C overnight with end-over-end
    mixing.

14. Spin-elute through a Spin-X column or similar to
    remove gel slices

    - 4 min at 7000 rpm

15. Purify using Zymo Clean & Concentrator-5 kit
    (R1015) [2,3]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 16000g.
    - Add 800.0 µL RNA binding buffer to the crude
      RNA.
    - Add 1200.0 µL 95-100% ethanol to the crude RNA.
    - Load on a Zymo-Spin IICR column.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.

    - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 700 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 30s; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 15 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

16. Make 5 µL, 2 µM aliquots.

Notes:
[1] Based on Fitzy's DNA PAGE purification protocol,
    [Nilson2013], and [Petrov2013].

[2] https://tinyurl.com/yd699u8b

[3] Column capacity: 10 µg
