 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    DNA template (p49) [nM]:   2   0     0     0    0   0   0   0
    mRNA template (f11) [nM]:  0  3300  1313  523  208  82  33  0

 3. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 4.98 μL 10000 nM f11 in a tube.
    - Put 3.00 μL nuclease-free water in 6 adjacent
      tubes.
    - Transfer 1.98 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 6 2.5-fold dilutions.

 4. Dilute the following stock solution to 6 nM [2]:

    Name  Stock Vol  Diluent Vol
    ────────────────────────────
    p49     2.00 µL     92.66 µL

 5. Setup 8 PUREfrex 1.0 (GeneFrontier PF001)
    reactions [3]:

    Reagent                       Stock   Volume      8.8x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    solution I                           1.25 µL  11.00 µL
    solution II                          0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    solution III                         0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine     40 U/µL  0.05 µL   0.44 µL
    FluoroTect GreenLys                  0.12 µL   1.10 µL
    p49,f11                    10000 nM  0.82 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         2.50 µL   1.68 µL/rxn

    - Thaw solution I at 25–37°C for 1 min, then keep
      on ice.
    - Thaw solutions II and III on ice.
    - Vortex all thawed solutions before use.

 6. Incubate at 37°C for 2h.

 7. Add 0.5 µL RNase cocktail (Invitrogen AM2286) to
    each reaction.

 8. Incubate at 37°C for 15 min.

 9. Prepare 8 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                Stock    Volume       12x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                          1.50 µL  18.00 µL
    TGSB                      2x   5.00 µL  60.00 µL
    NuPAGE reducing agent    10x   1.00 µL  12.00 µL
    sample                         2.50 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                  10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 85°C for 2 min.

10. Run a gel [4]:

    gel: 10–20% Novex tris-tricine SDS PAGE
    buffer: tricine SDS running buffer
    ladder: 5 µL SeeBlue Plus2 (Invitrogen LC5925)
    samples: 10 µL
    run: 125V for 90 min

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40
