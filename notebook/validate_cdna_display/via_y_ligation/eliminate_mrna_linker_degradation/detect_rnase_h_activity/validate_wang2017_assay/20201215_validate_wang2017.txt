$ stepwise validate_wang2017

 1. Prepare 100 U/mL RNase H:

    - 44 µL nuclease-free water
    - 5 µL 10x RNase H buffer
    - 1 µL 5000 U/mL RNase H

 2. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 17.55 μL 100.00 U/mL RNase H in the first tube.
    - Add 12.00 μL 1x RNase H reaction buffer in the 6 remaining tubes.
    - Transfer 5.55 μL between each tube to make 7
      3.2-fold dilutions.

 3. Prepare 5 µM o210-o212:

    - 1 µL 100 µM oligo
    - 19 µL nuclease-free water

 4. Setup 1 blank:

    - 10.0 µL 1x RNase H reaction buffer
    -  0.5 µL nuclease-free water [2]

 5. Setup 1 positive control:

    - 10.0 µL 1x RNase H reaction buffer
    -  0.5 µL 5 µM o212 [2]

 6. Setup 1 negative control:

    - 10.0 µL 100 U/mL RNase H
    -  0.5 µL 5 µM o211 [2]

 7. Setup 8 experimental samples:

    - 10.0 µL RNase H dilution, including 0 U/mL.
    -  0.5 µL 5 µM o210 [2]

 8. Incubate at 37°C for 120 min.

 9. Prepare 5x DNAzyme buffer:

    Reagent        Stock     Volume
    ───────────────────────────────
    water                 259.00 µL
    Tris pH=8.5  1000 mM  100.00 µL
    NaCl         5000 mM   40.00 µL
    MgCl₂        2000 mM    1.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────
                          400.00 µL

10. Setup the following reactions in a black, opaque-
    bottomed 96-well plate:

    Reagent               Stock     Volume      12.1x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water           69.40 µL  839.74 µL
    DNAzyme buffer           5x   20.00 µL  242.00 µL
    o213                 100 µM    0.10 µL    1.21 µL
    samples                       10.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 100.00 µL   89.50 µL/rxn

11. Measure flourescence (ex: 450, em: 521) in a
    plate reader for 30 min at 30°C without shaking.
