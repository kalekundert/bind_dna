$ stepwise 02_validate_rt_qpcr

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    template conc (nM):  100  100  10  1  0.1  0.01  0.001  0
    RT:                   −    +   +   +   +    +      +    +

 3. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 11.11 μL 100 nM f11 in a tube.

    - Put 10.00 μL nuclease-free water in 6 adjacent
      tubes.

    - Transfer 1.11 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 6 10-fold dilutions.

 4. Anneal the RT primer to the RNA template [2]:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume     8.8x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    first-strand buffer      5x  0.42 µL  3.70 µL
    f11                  100 nM  1.60 µL
    o214                 100 µM  0.08 µL  0.70 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 2.10 µL  0.50 µL/rxn

 5. Incubate at 70°C for 3 min, then immediately
    cool on ice.

 6. Setup 7 reverse transcription reactions:

    Reagent                        Stock    Volume      7.7x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                   10.45 µL  80.47 µL
    first-strand buffer               5x   3.74 µL  28.78 µL
    dNTP mix                       10 mM   2.00 µL  15.40 µL
    DTT                           100 mM   2.00 µL  15.40 µL
    SMART MMLV RT               200 U/µL   0.50 µL   3.85 µL
    annealed template/primer  76.1905 nM   1.31 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                          20.00 µL  18.69 µL/rxn

 7. Setup a −reverse transcriptase (NRT) control:

    Reagent                        Stock    Volume
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                   10.95 µL
    first-strand buffer               5x   3.74 µL
    dNTP mix                       10 mM   2.00 µL
    DTT                           100 mM   2.00 µL
    annealed template/primer  76.1905 nM   1.31 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────
                                          20.00 µL

 8. Incubate at 42°C for 60 min [3].

 9. Incubate at 70°C for 15 min.

10. Setup 8 qPCR reactions [4-6]:

    Reagent                Stock    Volume       8.8x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                         29.04 µL  255.55 µL
    RT products             5 nM   3.30 µL
    o214                  100 µM   0.33 µL    2.90 µL
    o215                  100 µM   0.33 µL    2.90 µL
    SsoAdvanced supermix      2x  33.00 µL  290.40 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                  66.00 µL   62.70 µL/rxn

    - Split each reaction into 2 tubes.
    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

11. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 95°C for 30s
    - Repeat 40x:
      - 95°C for 10s
      - 60°C for 15s
      - Measure fluorescence
    - 65.0-95.0°C in 5s steps of 0.5°C:
      - Measure fluorescence

Notes:
[1] The final concentrations will be:
    1  1.000000e+02 nM
    2  1.000000e+01 nM
    3  1.000000e+00 nM
    4  1.000000e-01 nM
    5  1.000000e-02 nM
    6  1.000000e-03 nM
    7  0.000000e+00 nM

[2] This protocol is based on the official Takara 
    SMART MMLV reverse transcription protocol:

    https://tinyurl.com/y4ash7dl

    However, I made three modifications:

    - I reduced the volume of the annealing step as 
      much as possible, the increase the 
      concentrations of the oligos.

    - I added buffer to the annealing step, because 
      annealing works much better with some salt to 
      shield the backbone charge.  I don't actually 
      know how much salt is in the buffer, but some is 
      better than none.

    - I included the MMLV in the transcription master 
      mix, because excluding it seemed too inaccurate.  
      The changes to the annealing step also mean that 
      the master mix has a 1x buffer concentration, so 
      I don't need to worry about the enzyme being 
      unhappy.

[3] Samples can be incubated for 50-90 min if
    necessary.

[4] https://tinyurl.com/y4qffgss

[5] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100.0 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[6] For diluting template DNA to 5 nM:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 1e+01*sqrt([DNA]) µL
