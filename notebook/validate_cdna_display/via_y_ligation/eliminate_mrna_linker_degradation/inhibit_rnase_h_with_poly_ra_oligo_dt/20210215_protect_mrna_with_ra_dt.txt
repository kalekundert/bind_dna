$ stepwise protect_mrna_with_ra_dt

 1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 2. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    PURExpress:                    −  −  −  −  +   +   +  +  +  +
    linker (o129, f89):            +  +  +  +  +   +   +  +  +  +
    mRNA (f89):                    −  −  +  +  +   +   +  +  +  +
    poly-rA/oligo-dT [A260 U/mL]:  0  8  0  8  0  0.5  1  2  4  8

 3. Dilute lyophilized poly-rA/oligo-dT (Midland
    P-4012) to 80 A260 U/mL:

    - 5 A260 U poly-rA/oligo-dT
    - 62.5 µL nuclease-free water

 4. Perform a serial dilution [1]:

    - Put 20.00 μL 8 U/mL poly-rA/oligo-dT in a tube.
    - Put 10.00 μL nuclease-free water in 5 adjacent
      tubes.
    - Transfer 10.00 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 5 2-fold dilutions.

 5. Setup 6 PURExpress (NEB E6800) reactions [2]:

    Reagent                           Stock   Volume      6.6x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                    0.10 µL   0.66 µL
    solution A                               2.00 µL  13.20 µL
    solution B                               1.50 µL   9.90 µL
    RNase inhibitor, murine         40 U/µL  0.10 µL   0.66 µL
    f89                             1000 nM  0.80 µL   5.28 µL
    poly-rA/oligo-dT         0-80 A260 U/mL  0.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                             5.00 µL   4.50 µL/rxn

    - Thaw all components on ice.
    - Mix A well; certain components may precipitate
      during storage.
    - Mix B gently; do not vortex.
    - Add A before B.

 6. Incubate at 37°C for 30 min.

 7. Setup 2 −mRNA controls:

    Reagent                     Stock   Volume     2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                3.70 µL  8.14 µL
    o129                         1 µM  0.80 µL  1.76 µL
    poly-rA/oligo-dT     80 A260 U/mL  0.50 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                       5.00 µL  4.50 µL/rxn

 8. Setup 2 −PURExpress controls:

    Reagent                     Stock   Volume     2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                3.70 µL  8.14 µL
    f89                          1 µM  0.80 µL  1.76 µL
    poly-rA/oligo-dT     80 A260 U/mL  0.50 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                       5.00 µL  4.50 µL/rxn

 9. Prepare 10 samples for SDS PAGE:

    Reagent                  Stock    Volume       15x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                            3.93 µL  58.95 µL
    Bolt LDS sample buffer      4x   2.50 µL  37.50 µL
    Bolt reducing agent        10x   1.00 µL  15.00 µL
    o194                    100 µM   0.07 µL   1.05 µL
    sample                    7 nM   2.50 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                    10.00 µL   7.50 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 10 min.

10. Run a gel:

    - Gel: 4−12% Bolt Bis-Tris SDS PAGE
    - Buffer: Bolt MES SDS running buffer
    - Load 10 µL of each sample.
    - Run at 165V for 42 min.

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40
