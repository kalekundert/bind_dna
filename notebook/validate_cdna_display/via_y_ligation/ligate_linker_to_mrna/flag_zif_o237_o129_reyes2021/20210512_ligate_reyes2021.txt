$ sw ligate_reyes2021

 1. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    FLAG (f111, o239):     +  +  −  −
    Zif268 (f11, o129):    −  −  +  +
    established protocol:  +  −  +  −
    Reyes2021 protocol:    −  +  −  +

 2. Setup 2 ligation reactions:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume     2.2x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water          2.80 µL  6.16 µL
    T4 RNA ligase buffer    10x  1.00 µL  2.20 µL
    ATP                   10 mM  1.00 µL  2.20 µL
    f11,f111              10 µM  2.00 µL
    o129,o239             10 µM  3.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 9.80 µL  4.80 µL/rxn

 3. Incubate as follows:

    - 90°C for 30s
    - Cool to 25°C at 1°C/s

 4. Add to each reaction:

    - 0.2 µL 10 U/µL T4 RNA ligase

 5. Incubate at 25°C for 30 min.

 6. Label the products: f119, f115

 7. Setup 2 annealing reactions [1]:

    Reagent    Stock   Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    f11,f111   10 µM  2.00 µL
    o129,o239  10 µM  1.20 µL
    PBS          10x  0.36 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                      3.56 µL

 8. Perform the annealing reactions:

    - Incubate at 95°C for 2 min.
    - Cool at room temperature.

 9. Setup 1 ligation reaction:

    Reagent                       Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                  24.69 µL
    T4 RNA ligase buffer            10x   3.56 µL
    ATP                           10 mM   3.56 µL
    T4 RNA ligase (NEB M0204)   10 U/µL   0.20 µL
    annealed mRNA/linker       5.625 µM   3.56 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         35.56 µL

10. Incubate the ligation reaction as follows:

    - 25°C for 10 min.
    - 65°C for 10 min.

11. Label the products: f119, f115

12. Prepare 4 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent                    Stock    Volume        6x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    formamide loading buffer      2x   5.00 µL  30.00 µL
    o194                      100 µM   0.56 µL   3.36 µL
    sample                    125 nM   4.44 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                      10.00 µL   5.56 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

13. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL low range ssRNA ladder (NEB N0364)
    samples: 10 µL
    run: 180V for 40 min

14. Stain gel with GelGreen [2-4]:

    - Keep the stain protected from light.

    - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM
      NaCl.
    - Shake gently for 30m.

15. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:   488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40

Notes:
[1] Using 0.6x linker reduces the amount of unligated
    linker, see expt #1.

[2] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[3] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[4] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
