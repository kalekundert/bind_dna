$ sw ligate_with_ribozyme

 1. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    ribozyme:          −  67  67  67  56  56  56
    mRNA (f123,f124):  −  +   +   +   +   +   +
    linker (o129):     +  −   +   +   −   +   +
    ligase:            −  −   −   +   −   −   +

 2. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 3. Prepare the 750 nM linker-only control:

    - 0.5 µL 10 µM o129
    - 6.17 µL nuclease-free water

 4. Prepare the 500 nM mRNA-only controls:

    - 2 µL 10 µM f123,f124
    - 2 µL nuclease-free water

 5. Setup 2 annealing reactions [1]:

    Reagent               Stock   Volume     2.2x
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    T4 RNA ligase buffer    10x  0.56 µL  1.22 µL
    f123,f124              1 µM  4.35 µL
    o129                  10 µM  0.65 µL  1.43 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 5.56 µL  1.21 µL/rxn

 6. Incubate as follows:

    - 90°C for 30s
    - Cool to 25°C at 1°C/s

 7. Setup 2 ligation reactions:

    Reagent                 Stock   Volume    ≈6.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water            0.82 µL  5.12 µL
    T4 RNA ligase buffer      10x  0.14 µL  0.90 µL
    ATP                     10 mM  0.40 µL  2.50 µL
    T4 RNA ligase         10 U/µL  0.08 µL  0.50 µL
    annealed mRNA/linker           2.56 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
                                   4.00 µL  1.44 µL/rxn

 8. Incubate as follows:

    - 25°C for 10 min
    - 65°C for 10 min

 9. Prepare the −ligase controls:

    - 2.56 µL annealed mRNA/linker
    - 1.44 µL nuclease-free water

10. Prepare 7 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent               Stock    Volume     10.5x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease free water           0.20 µL   2.10 µL
    FESV                     2x   5.00 µL  52.50 µL
    o194                 100 µM   1.60 µL  16.80 µL
    sample               500 nM   3.20 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 10.00 µL   6.80 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

11. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    ladder: 1 µL low range ssRNA ladder (NEB N0364S)
    samples: 10 µL/lane
    run: 180V for 40 min

12. Stain gel with GelGreen [2-4]:

    - Keep the stain protected from light.

    - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM
      NaCl.
    - Shake gently for 30m.

13. Image with a laser scanner:

    lasers:    488 nm   658 nm
    filters:  518BP22  710BP40

Notes:
[1] Using 0.6x linker reduces the amount of unligated
    linker, see expt #1

[2] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[3] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[4] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
