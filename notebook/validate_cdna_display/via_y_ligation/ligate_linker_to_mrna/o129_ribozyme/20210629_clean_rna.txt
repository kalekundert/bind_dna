1. Resuspend each vial of lyophilized DNase I (Zymo
   E1009-A, 250 U) in 275 µL nuclease-rfee water.
   Mix by gentle inversion.  Store frozen aliquots.

2. Setup 2 DNase reactions [1]:

   Reagent                Stock     Volume       2.2x
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                         140.00 µL  308.00 µL
   DNA digestion buffer     10x   20.00 µL   44.00 µL
   DNase I               1 U/µL   20.00 µL   44.00 µL
   IVT reaction          1 U/µL   20.00 µL
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 200.00 µL  180.00 µL/rxn

3. Incubate at room temperature for 15 min.

4. Purify using Zymo Clean & Concentrator-100 kit
   (R1019) [2,3]:

   - Perform all spin steps at 16000g.
   - Add 2 volumes RNA binding buffer to the crude
     RNA.
   - Add 3 volumes 95-100% ethanol to the crude RNA.
   - Load on a Zymo-Spin V-E column with reservoir.
   - Apply vacuum.

   - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
   - Apply vacuum.
   - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
   - Apply vacuum.
   - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
   - Apply vacuum.
   - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

   - Add 100 µL nuclease-free water.
   - Spin 30s; keep flow-through.

Notes:
[1] From Zymo Clean & Concentrator manual
