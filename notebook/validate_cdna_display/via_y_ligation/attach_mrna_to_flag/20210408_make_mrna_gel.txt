$ sw cdna/make_mrna f111 o237
$ sw gels/gel urea 2

 1. Setup 1 annealing reaction [1]:

    Reagent  Stock   Volume
    ───────────────────────
    PBS        10x  0.89 µL
    f111     10 µM  5.00 µL
    o237     10 µM  3.00 µL
    ───────────────────────
                    8.89 µL

 2. Perform the annealing reaction:

    - Incubate at 95°C for 2 min.
    - Cool at room temperature.

 3. Setup 1 ligation reaction:

    Reagent                       Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water                  59.72 µL
    T4 RNA ligase buffer            10x   8.89 µL
    ATP                           10 mM   8.89 µL
    T4 RNA ligase (NEB M0204)   10 U/µL   2.50 µL
    annealed mRNA/linker       5.625 µM   8.89 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────
                                         88.89 µL

 4. Incubate the ligation reaction as follows:

    - 25°C for 10 min.
    - 65°C for 10 min.

 5. Remove unligated linker by ultrafiltration:

    - Bring reaction to 500 µL with 8M urea.
    - Load onto a 100 kDa MWCO spin-filter [2].
    - Spin 14000g, 15 min.
    - Wash with 500 µL 8M urea.
    - Wash with 500 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Wash with water again.
    - Wash with water again, but spin for 30 min [2].
    - Invert the filter into a clean tube and spin
      1000g, 2 min to collect ligated product in a
      volume of ≈15 µL.

 6. Dilute the purified mRNA to 1 µM.

 7. Make 4 µL, 1 µM aliquots.

 8. Prepare 2 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

    Reagent            Stock    Volume        3x
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                      4.00 µL  12.00 µL
    sample buffer         2x   5.00 µL  15.00 µL
    sample         200 ng/µL   1.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────────
                              10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

    - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

 9. Run a gel:

    gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
    buffer: 1x TBE
    samples: 10 µL
    run: 180V for 40 min

10. Stain gel with GelGreen [4-6]:

    - Keep the stain protected from light.

    - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM
      NaCl.
    - Shake gently for 30m.

11. Image with a laser scanner:

    laser:   488 nm
    filter:  518BP22

Notes:
[1] Using 0.6x linker reduces the amount of unligated
    linker, see expt #1.

[2] Amicon UFC510024

[3] Final urea concentration: ≈200 µM

[4] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[5] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[6] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
