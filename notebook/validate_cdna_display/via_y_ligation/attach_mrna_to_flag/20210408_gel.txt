$ sw gels/gel urea 2

1. Prepare 2 samples for TBE/urea PAGE:

   Reagent            Stock    Volume        3x
   ────────────────────────────────────────────
   water                      4.00 µL  12.00 µL
   sample buffer         2x   5.00 µL  15.00 µL
   sample         200 ng/µL   1.00 µL
   ────────────────────────────────────────────
                             10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

   - Incubate at 70°C for 3 min.

2. Run a gel:

   gel: 6% TBE/urea PAGE
   buffer: 1x TBE
   samples: 10 µL
   run: 180V for 40 min

3. Stain gel with GelGreen [1-3]:

   - Keep the stain protected from light.

   - Submerge gel in ≈30 mL 3x GelGreen, 100 mM NaCl.
   - Shake gently for 30m.

4. Image with a laser scanner:

   laser:   488 nm
   filter:  518BP22

Notes:
[1] https://tinyurl.com/etxhfxws

[2] Including 100 mM NaCl in the staining solution
    enhances sensitivity, but may promote dye
    precipitation if the gel stain is reused.

[3] Staining solution can be reused at least 2-3x.
    Store staining solution at room temperature
    protected from light.
