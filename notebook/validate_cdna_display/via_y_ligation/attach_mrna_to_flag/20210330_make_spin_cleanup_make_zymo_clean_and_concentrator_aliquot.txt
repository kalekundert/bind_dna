$ sw make f110
$ sw spin_cleanup
$ sw make f111
$ sw zymo_clean_and_concentrator
$ sw aliquot '5 µL' '10 µM'

 1. Prepare 10x primer mix [1]:

    Reagent   Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────
    water             9.00 µL
    o231     100 µM   0.50 µL
    o232     100 µM   0.50 µL
    ─────────────────────────
                     10.00 µL

 2. Setup 1 PCR reaction [2,3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────
    water                    15.00 µL
    f109           20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
    primer mix          10x   5.00 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  25.00 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────
                             50.00 µL

 3. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 64°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 10s
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 4. Label the product: f110

 5. Purify using Qiagen MinElute PCR purification kit
    (28004) [4]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Add 5 volumes PB to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 0.2 volumes 3M sodium
      acetate, pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 6. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch
    (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

 7. Setup 1 in vitro transcription reaction [5,6]:

    Reagent                  Stock    Volume
    ────────────────────────────────────────
    nuclease-free water              6.50 µL
    reaction buffer            10x   1.50 µL
    rNTP mix                100 mM   6.00 µL
    HiScribe T7                10x   2.00 µL
    f110                 250 ng/µL   4.00 µL
    ────────────────────────────────────────
                                    20.00 µL

    - Mix reagents in the order given.
    - Ok to handle at room temperature.

 8. Incubate at 37°C for 4 hours [7].

 9. Label the product: f111

10. Purify RNA using Zymo Clean & Concentrator spin
    columns:

    - If necessary, bring each sample to 50 µL.
    - Add 2 volumes RNA binding buffer; mix.
    - Add 3 volumes >95% ethanol; mix.
    - Load sample on spin column.
    - Spin 1 min, 16000g; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA prep buffer.
    - Spin 30s, 16000g; discard flow-through.
    - Add 700 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 30s, 16000g; discard flow-through.
    - Add 400 µL RNA wash buffer.
    - Spin 60s, 16000g; discard flow-through.
    - Add 25 µL nuclease-free water.
    - Spin 30s, 16000g.

11. Make 5 µL, 10 µM aliquots.

Notes:
[1] For resuspending lyophilized primers:
    100.0 µM = 10 µL/nmol

[2] https://tinyurl.com/y27ralt4

[3] For diluting template DNA to 20 pg/µL:
    Dilute 1 µL twice into 7*sqrt([DNA]) µL

[4] https://tinyurl.com/59wynv64

[5] https://tinyurl.com/y4a2j8w5

[6] I've found that T7 kits which have been in the
    freezer for more than ≈4 weeks seem to produce
    more degraded RNA.

[7] Use a thermocycler to prevent evaporation.
