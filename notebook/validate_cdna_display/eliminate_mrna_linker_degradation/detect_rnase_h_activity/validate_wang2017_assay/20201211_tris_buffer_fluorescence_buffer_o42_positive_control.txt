$ stepwise recipes/tris_buffer 8.5 -v 50
$ stepwise fluorescence_buffer
$ stepwise o42_positive_control

1. Prepare 1000.0 mM pH=8.5 Tris buffer:

   - 2.21 g Tris HCl
   - 4.36 g Tris base
   - water to 50.0 mL

2. Prepare 1 mL 10x fluorescence buffer [1]:

   - 500 µL 1M   (20x) Tris pH=8.5
   - 200 µL 5M   (50x) NaCl 
   -   5 µL 2M (2000x) MgCl₂

3. Prepare positive and negative controls:

   - 10 µL 10x fluorescence buffer
   - 2 µL 5 µM o42 (positive control) or
          water (negative control)
   - 88 µL water

4. Measure fluorescence (ex: 450, em: 521) in a plate 
   reader for 30 min at 30°C without shaking.

Note:
[1] DOI: 10.1021/acs.analchem.7b02899
