1. Perform a serial dilution [2]:

   - Put 35.10 μL 10.00 U/mL RNase H in the first tube.
   - Add 24.00 μL 1x RNase H reaction buffer in the 6 remaining tubes.
   - Transfer 11.10 μL between each tube to make 7
     3.2-fold dilutions.

2. Prepare 5 µM dilutions of o210-o212.

3. Setup 1 blank:

   - 10.0 µL 1x RNase H reaction buffer
   -  0.5 µL water [2]

4. Setup 1 positive control:

   - 10.0 µL 1x RNase H reaction buffer
   -  0.5 µL 5 µM o212 [2]

5. Setup 1 negative control:

   - 10.0 µL 10 U/mL RNase H
   -  0.5 µL 5 µM o211 [2]

6. Setup 8 experimental samples:

   - 10.0 µL RNase H dilution, including 0 U/mL.
   -  0.5 µL 5 µM o210 [2]

7. Incubate at 30°C for 10 min.

   bottomed 96-well plate:

   Reagent        Stock     Volume      12.1x

   reader for 30 min at 30°C without shaking.
