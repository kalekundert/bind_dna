- Prepare buffer B [2]:

  - 100 mM (13.8 g/L) NaH₂PO₄
  - 10 mM (1.2 g/L) Tris Cl
  - 8M (480.5 g/L) urea
  - Adjust pH to 8.0 using NaOH

- Prepare buffer C [2]:

  - 100 mM (13.8 g/L) NaH₂PO₄
  - 10 mM (1.2 g/L) Tris Cl
  - 8M (480.5 g/L) urea
  - Adjust pH to 6.3 using HCl

- Prepare buffer E [2]:

  - 100 mM (13.8 g/L) NaH₂PO₄
  - 10 mM (1.2 g/L) Tris Cl
  - 8M (480.5 g/L) urea
  - Adjust pH to 4.5 using HCl

- Resuspend cells in 400 µL buffer B.
  - Keep 10 µL for SDS PAGE.

- Lyse cells by vortexing gently (e.g. taking care 
  to avoid frothing).  The solutions should become 
  translucent.

- Centrifuge the lysates for 20-30 min at 15,000g.  
  Transfer supernatants to a clean tube.
  - Keep 10 µL for SDS PAGE.

- Purify the lysates using Ni-NTA spin columns:

  - Load 600 µL buffer B.
  - Centrifuge for 2 min at 700g.

  - Load the cleared lysate.
  - Centrifuge for 2 min at 700g [3].
  - Keep 10 µL for SDS PAGE.

  - Repeat 1-3x [4]:
    - Load 600 µL buffer C.
    - Centrifuge for 2 min at 700g.
    - Keep 10 µL for SDS PAGE.

  - Repeat 1-2x [5]:
    - Load 200 µL buffer E.
    - Centrifuge for 2 min at 700g.
    - Keep 10 µL for SDS PAGE.
    - Combine the remaining eluate.

Notes:

[1] Protocol 5: Rapid screening of small 
    expression cultures.  The QIAexpressionist 
    (2003), pg. 45
