 1. Setup 2 PCR reactions [1-3]:

    Reagent           Stock    Volume      2.2x
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
    water                    19.50 µL  42.90 µL
    p18,p45        20 pg/µL   5.00 µL
    o34              100 µM   0.25 µL   0.55 µL
    o35              100 µM   0.25 µL   0.55 µL
    Q5 master mix        2x  25.00 µL  55.00 µL
    ───────────────────────────────────────────
                             50.00 µL  45.00 µL/rxn

    - Use any extra master mix as a negative control.

 2. Run the following thermocycler protocol:

    - 98°C for 30s
    - Repeat 35x:
      - 98°C for 10s
      - 60°C for 20s
      - 72°C for 10s
    - 72°C for 2 min
    - 4°C hold

 3. Label the products: f128, f129

 4. Purify using Qiagen MinElute reaction cleanup kit
    (28204) [4,5]:

    - Perform all spin steps at 17900g.
    - Ensure that the sample is between 20–100 µL.
    - Add 300 µL ERC to the crude DNA.
    - If not yellow: Add 10 µL 3M sodium acetate,
      pH=5.0.
    - Load on a MinElute column.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.

    - Add 750 µL PE.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Spin 1m; discard flow-through.
    - Add 10.0 µL EB.
    - Wait at least 1m.
    - Spin 1m; keep flow-through.

 5. Dilute f128 and f129 to 100 nM.

 6. Prepare 40 µL 5x Zif268 binding buffer:
    
    Reagent                   Stock    1x Conc  5x Volume
    ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────
    water                                         12.4 µL
    Zif268 storage buffer       10x         1x    20.0 µL
    DTT                      500 mM       5 mM     2.0 µL
    BSA                    10 mg/mL  200 µg/mL     4.0 µL
    ssDNA                  10 mg/mL   80 µg/mL     1.6 µL

 7. In the following steps, setup these conditions:

    target:           +   −   +  −  +  −  +  −  +  −   +    −   +  −
    Zif268 [excess]:  16  16  8  8  4  4  2  2  1  1  0.5  0.5  0  0

 8. Perform a serial dilution [6]:

    - Put 20.00 μL 1600 nM PCV2-Zif268 in a tube.
    - Put 10.00 μL 1x Zif268 storage buffer in 6
      adjacent tubes.
    - Transfer 10.00 μL between each tube *except the
      last* to make 6 2-fold dilutions.

 9. Setup 7 binding reactions:

    Reagent          Stock    Volume      7.7x
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
    water                    6.00 µL  46.20 µL
    binding buffer      5x   2.00 µL  15.40 µL
    f128,f129       100 nM   1.00 µL   7.70 µL
    PCV2-Zif268              1.00 µL
    ──────────────────────────────────────────
                            10.00 µL   9.00 µL/rxn

    - Make a separate master mix for each target.

10. Incubate at room temperature for 1h.

11. Prepare 14 samples for native PAGE:

    Reagent  Stock   Volume       21x
    ─────────────────────────────────
    water           3.25 µL  68.25 µL
    BTSGV       4x  1.50 µL  31.50 µL
    sample          1.25 µL
    ─────────────────────────────────
                    6.00 µL   4.75 µL/rxn

12. Run a gel:

    gel: 4–16% Bis-Tris Novex NativePAGE
    buffer: NativePAGE running buffer
    samples: 5 µL/lane
    run: 150V for 115 min

13. Image with a laser scanner:

    laser:    658 nm
    filter:  710BP40
