1. Wipe down your bench and anything you'll touch 
   (pipets, racks, pens, etc.) with RNaseZap.

2. Setup 4 in vitro transcription reactions by 
   mixing the following reagents at room temperature 
   in the order given.

   Reagent                 Conc  Each Rxn  Master Mix
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
   nuclease-free water            3.50 μL    15.40 μL
   reaction buffer          10x   2.00 μL     8.80 μL
   RNase inhibitor      40 U/μL   0.50 μL     2.20 μL
   rNTP mix              100 mM   8.00 μL    35.20 μL
   HiScribe T7              10x   2.00 μL     8.80 μL
   DNA template         5 ng/μL   4.00 μL            
   ──────────────────────────────────────────────────
                                 20.00 μL    16.00 μL/rxn

3. Incubate at 37°C (thermocycler) for 2 hours.

4. Remove unincorporated ribonucleotides using Zymo 
   RNA Clean & Concentrator 25 spin columns.

5. Nanodrop to determine the RNA concentration.

6. Dilute (if desired) enough RNA to make several 
   15 μL aliquots and to run a gel.  Keep any left- 
   over RNA undiluted.  Flash-freeze in liquid N₂ and 
   store at -80°C.

7. Run the RNA on a denaturing gel to make sure it's 
   homogeneous and of the right size.
